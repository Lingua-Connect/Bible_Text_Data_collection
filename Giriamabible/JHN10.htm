10
Fumbo ra murisa na ng'ondzize
1 Bai Jesu akiamba, “Ninamwambira ujeri wenye kukala ye asiyengira chaani na muryangoni, ela anakwera na dzulu, be iye ni mwivi kidza ni jambazi. 2 Ela ye angiraye na muryangoni nde murisa wa zo ng'ondzi. 3 Ye murindzi ni kumuvugulira muryango ye murisa, na zo ng'ondzi nikusikira sauthiye kalaho anaziha na madzina na kuzilongoza ndze. 4 10:4 10:27; Ugun 14:4Akizamboza ndze, iye nikuzithangulia na zo ng'ondzi ni kumuthuwa, kwa kukala zinaimanya yo sauthiye. 5 Ng'ondzi zizo k'azidima kumuthuwa mut'u yoyosi mungine, hat'uhe zindamuchimbira ye mut'u, kwa kukala k'aziimanya iyo sauthiye.” 6 10:6 16:25Jesu waambira fumbo riri, ela k'amamanyire mana ya zho arizhokala anaambira.
Jesu nde murisa mudzo
7 Kwa vizho Jesu akinena nao kaheri, akiamba, “Ninamwambira ujeri wenye, mimi ndimi muryango wa zizo ng'ondzi. 8 10:8 Jere 23:1-2; Izik 34:2-3Osini mariokudza mbere zangu were ni aivi na majambazi, ela zo ng'ondzi k'azisirikizire zizo sauthi zao bule. 9 10:9 14:6; Zabu 23:2; Izik 34:14Mimi ndimi muryango. Kila mut'u andiyengira kukirira kwangu andaokoka, andangira na kidza andambola na apate ndiso. 10 Mwivi akidza hat'u, nikukala adzire aiye, alage na anonge-nonge, ela mimi bai nakudza kwamba mupate uzima, na muupate thele kwa thele.
11 10:11 Murisa: Zabu 23:1; Izik 34:11-16; Jere 23:3; Math' 18:12-14; Aebu 13:20; Ugun 7:17; Kufwa: 15:13; Arum 5:8; 1 Joh 3:16“Mimi ndimi murisa mudzo. Murisa mudzo yu thayari kufwa kwa ut'u wa zo ng'ondzi. 12 10:12 Mahe 20:29Ela mut'u wa kwandhikwa na ariye si murisa mudzo na kidza ariye zo ng'ondzi si zakwe, aonaho mbawa ni kuziricha zo ng'ondzi, na akachimbira. Bai mbawa ni kuzigwira zo ng'ondzi na nyingine zikatsamukana. 13 Ye wa kwandhikwa ni kuchimbira kwa kukala ni mwandhikwa na k'azijali bule zo ng'ondzi.
14-15 10:14 10:27; 2 T'imo 2:1910:15 Math' 11:27“Mimi ndimi murisa mudzo. Here dza Baba animanyazho mimi, nami namumanya iye, bai vizho ndo nami nimanyazho zo ng'ondzi zangu, nazo kidza zinanimanya mimi. Nami ni thayari kufwa kwa ut'u wa zizo. 16 10:16 11:52; Isa 56:8; Izik 34:23-24; 1 P'et 2:25Na kuna ng'ondzi zangu nyingine ambazo k'azi kahi za chaa kiki bule. Bai zizo nazo ninav̱irya nizirehe. Ng'ondzi zizo zindasikira sauthi yangu, na zindakala badhi mwenga na renye murisa mumwenga. 17 10:17 3:35; 5:18Iye Baba ananihendza kwa kukala ni thayari kulazha uzima wangu ili nidime kuuhokera kaheri. 18 10:18 4:34K'akuna adimaye kuuhala kula kwangu uwo uzima wangu, ela ninahenda kuulazha kwa kuhendza mwenye. Nina wadimi wa kuulazha uzima wangu na nina wadimi wa kuuhala mwenye kaheri.”
19 10:19 9:16Marihosikira vivi o Ayahudi magazhikana kaheri. 20 10:20 8:48Anji ao were manaamba, “Mut'u yuyu ana p'ep'o na kidza ana vilalu, munamusirikiza wani benwi?”
21 Ela angine were manaamba, “Mut'u ariye na p'ep'o k'adima kunena dza vivi. Adimadze p'ep'o kuhenda vipofu maone?”
Vilongozi a Kiyahudi ma kinyume cha Jesu
22 Wakathi wa sikuk'uu ya Kukumbukira Kuhendywa Eri kwa ro Hekalu kuko Jerusalemu wafika. Nao wakathi uwo were ni wakathi wa mwaka. 23 10:23 Mahe 3:11Naye Jesu were anatsembera mo Hekaluni kahi za baraza riifwaro ra Selemani. 24 10:24 Luk 22:67K'uzhona zho Ayahudi makimuzingira na makiamba, “We undahuhendya huaze-aze hatha rini? Kala ndiwe Masihi fwambire lwazu-lwazu thu.”
25 10:25 5:36Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “At'u ninwi namwambira kare, ela k'amukuluhirire. Bai zo kazi zangu nihendazo kwa dzina ra Baba zinaninenera, zizo vizho zinanimanyisa kukala ni h'ani.
26 10:26 16:9“Ela k'amukuluhira kwa kukala k'amu kahi za badhi rangu. 27 10:27 8:47; 10:3, 14Mut'u mimi ninazimanya zo ng'ondzi zangu, nazo nikusikira sauthi yangu na zikanithuwa, 28 10:28 5:24; 6:39nami ninazigera uzima wa kare na kare, nazo k'azindafwa na k'akuna adimaye kunihoka bule. 29 Iye Baba ariyenip'a ni mubomu kuriko osi na k'akuna adimaye kuzinyagula kula mikononi mwa Baba. 30 10:30 5:18; 17:21Mimi na Baba hu kit'u kimwenga.”
31 10:31 11:8Kusikira vizho o Ayahudi makitsola mawe kaheri mamukot'e. 32 Jesu akiambira, “Nidzahenda mahendo manji madzo mbere zenu ambago Baba waniamba nigahende. Munamala kunikot'a mawe kwa hiro kahi za go?”
33 10:33 Alaw 24:16; Math' 26:65O Ayahudi makimudzigidzya makiamba, “K'ahumala kukukot'a mawe kwa mahendo madzo gogosi, ela kwa viryahu urizho unamukufuru Mulungu! Uwe u mudamu thu, ela unamala kudzihendya Mulungu.”
34 10:34 Zabu 82:6Jesu akidzigidzya akiamba, “Zhaorwa kahi za Sheria yenu kukala Mulungu waambira angine, ‘Ninwi mu milungu.’ 35 Mulungu waaiha milungu aryahu ambao map'ewa uv̱oro na hunamanya kukala zho gaambazho go Maoro ni ujeri hatha kare na kare. 36 10:36 1:34Vidze munamwamba iye ariyetsagulwa ni Baba na akihumwa urumwenguni, kukala anakufuru Mulungu kwa kukala naamba, ‘Ni Mwana wa Mulungu’? 37 Kala sihenda kazi za Baba, k'ahana haja ya kunikuluhira. 38 10:38 10:25Ela kala ninazihenda, ingahokala k'amunikuluhira munav̱irya kukuluhira zo kazi zangu, ili mumanye kamare-kamare kukala Baba yu ndani yangu, na mimi ni ndaniye.”
39 10:39 7:30; 8:20K'uzhona zho kusikira vizho makijeza kumugwira kaheri, ela akihenyeza mikononi mwao.
40 10:40 1:28Jesu akivuka kaheri nyangamo ya muho wa Jorodani hatha hat'u haryahu arihokala Johana wabat'iza at'u na akikala haho. 41 10:41 1:29-34; 3:27-28Na lola at'u anji makimudzirira makiamba, “Johana k'ahendere miujiza mibomu, ela gosi arigonena dzulu za mut'u yuyu were ni jeri.” 42 10:42 2:23Bai at'u anji kuko makimukuluhira Jesu.
