3
Johana Mubat'izaji anahubiri na kubat'iza at'u
Mariko 1:1-8; Luka 3:1-20; Johana 1:19-28
1 Bai bada ya miaka ya kuthala, kwambola mwanamulume ariyekala anaifwa Johana Mubat'izaji. Johana wahubiri ko nyika ya Judea na kuamba, 2 3:2 4:17; Luk 3:3“Thubuni, kwani uthawali wa mulunguni udzafika hehi.” 3 3:3 Isa 40:3Yuyu Johana ndiye ariyeambwa ni nabii Isaya haryahu arihonena akiamba,
“Mut'u analuta mumirowe ko nyika na kuamba,
‘Mutengezereni ngira Bwana,
Mutengezereni ngira ya kugoloka.’ ”
4 3:4 2 Athaw 1:8Valo ra Johana were ni ra nyoga za ngamira. Na wakala akidzifunga ukanda wa k'ingo chununi mwakwe. Chakuryache were ni kw'eth'eth'e na uchi wa nyuchi. 5 K'uzhona zho, at'u osi a Jerusalemu na a midzi mingine yosi ya Judea, na at'u osi mariokala makitoa kahi za midzi iriyo k'anda-k'anda za muho wa Jorodani marihovisikira, bagabaga makimuthuwa. 6 Nao maziungama dambi zao, na Johana akiabat'iza kahi za wo muho wa Jorodani. 7 3:7 Kizhazi cha nyoka: 12:34; 23:33; K'oro: 24:15-25, 37-39Ela arihokala anaenderera kubat'iza, kukidza Mafarisayo na Masadukayo anji, nao makikala madzirebat'izwa. Bai Johana arihoaona, akiadumulira akiamba, “H'enwi kizhazi cha nyoka nwi! Ni h'ani ariyemwambira kukala muzichimbire zo k'oro za Mulungu zidzazo? 8 H'aya mahendo genu naganyese kukala mudzathubu dambi jeri. 9 3:9 Luk 1:54-55; Joh 8:39; Arum 2:28-29; 4:12Na musidzikenge bule mukaamba mu ana a Burahemu. Kwani namwambira kamare kukala, Mulungu anadima kumuumbira ana Burahemu hatha kula kwa gaga mawe! 10 3:10 7:19Na vivi be tsoka ri ho kolo kare, na kila muhi usiwozhala matunda madzo, undathemwa utsufwe mohoni. 11 3:11 Luk 3:16Mino ninamubat'iza kwa madzi kamwe, kunyesa kukala mudzathubu dambi zenu, ela ye andiyekudza nyuma zangu ni mubomu kukira mimi. Kwandza mosi sifika hatha mut'u wa kumuvula virahuzhe. Iye andamubat'iza kwa Roho Mweri na kwa moho. 12 3:12 Jere 13:24; Math' 13:30Na adzagwirira lungo anahehet'a ngano. Akigonya kuhehet'a andahala yo ngano ayangize lutsagani, gonya ahale wo wiswa autsuhe kahi za moho usiwozima.”
Kubat'izwa kwa Jesu
Mariko 1:9-11; Luka 3:21-22
13 Bai makathi garyahu Johana arihokala anabat'iza at'u, Jesu wambola jimbo ra Galilaya achenda Jorodani ili naye akabat'izwe ni Johana. 14 Vikara Johana arihoona kukala Jesu naye anamala kubat'izwa akimwamba, “Mino namala nibat'izwe ni uwe, we unadza kwangu?” Kwa vizho akikala anamala kukahala kumubat'iza. 15 3:15 5:17; 28:19Ela Jesu akimudzigidzya akimwamba, “Vihende kwa rero, kwani vizho ndo hundakala hunathimiza hachi yosi.” Johana arihosikira viryahu akikubali kumubat'iza Jesu. 16 3:16 Mulunguni kukigunuka: Isa 63:19; Izik 1:1-3; Joh 1:51; Mahe 7:56Bai, Jesu arihokala adzagonya kubat'izwa wambola mo madzini. Bai arihombola thu, lola ko mulunguni kukigunuka, na akiona Roho wa Mulungu anatharamuka here giya hatha akidzamuthulama. 17 3:17 Isa 42:1; Zabu 2:7; Math' 12:18; 17:5Na lola haho henye mumiro uchambola kula ko mulunguni ukiamba, “Yuyu ni Mwanangu muhendzwi, anifwahizaye.”
