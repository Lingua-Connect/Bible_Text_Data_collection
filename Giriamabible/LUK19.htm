19
Jesu na Zakayo
1 Jesu achangira mudzi wa Jeriko, naye akikala anakira kahikahi za wo mudzi. 2 Bai hakikala hana mut'u ariye were ni mubomu wa athozi a kodi dzinare were ni Zakayo, naye were ni shaha k'azhadimikika. 3 Naye were anamala kumona Jesu amanye chamba ni mut'u ariyedze, ela vikimushinda mana were ni mut'u mufuti mwenye na ro kundi ra at'u rere rinamuziya. 4 Kwa vizho akiuka malo achenda ko mbere, na achendakwera mukuyu ili adime kumona Jesu, kwani Jesu were andakira na ngira iyo.
5 Bai Jesu arihofika ho hat'u haho, walola dzulu na akimwamba Zakayo, “Zakayo, thima mara mwenga! Kwani vivi rero be suthi nikale mujenio.” 6 Kusikira vizho Zakayo wathima mara mwenga na akimuhala Jesu achenda naye kwakwe mudzini kuno ana raha ut'u wa kwangalaza. 7 19:7 Math' 9:10Ela o at'u, ut'u uu k'auafwahirire bule, kwani makwatya kunung'unika na kuamba, “Mut'u yuyu adzakwenda ujeni kwa mut'u ariye be ni mudambi zhomu!” 8 19:8 Kumbo 22:1; Izik 33:14-16K'uzhona zho Zakayo akiungarara na akimwamba Bwana vivi, “Lola Bwana! Kulaiza virero be nusu ya ushaha wangu nindaip'a akiya, na mut'u yoyosi niriyemukenga na nikimuthoza kiwango kibomu, be mut'u iye nindamuriha k'ana ne zenye ya zho nirizhomuhalira.” 9 19:9 13:16; Mahe 16:31Jesu akimwamba Zakayo, “Rero wokolwi udzangira kahi za nyumba ii, kwani yuyu naye ni wa lukolo lwa Burahemu. 10 19:10 Izik 34:16; Math' 9:13; Mar 2:17; Luk 5:32; 1 T'imo 1:15Na Mwana wa Mudamu wakudza kuamala na kuakola aryahu mariongamika.”
Fumbo ra at'u mariogazhirwa p'esa
Math'ayo 25:14-30
11 19:11 11:2; Mahe 1:6Bai o at'u marihokala machere manaaza go maut'u gaga, Jesu waenderera kunena na akiambira fumbo, kwa kukala were Jesu a hehi na mudzi wa Jerusalemu na o at'u makiona kukala wo uthawali wa Mulungu be undahenda kuhenda poo. 12 19:12-13 Mar 13:34; Mahe 1:11; 2:33-36Kwa vizho Jesu akikwatya kunena ro fumbo akiamba, “Kwereko na mut'u ariyekala wazhalwa kahi za nyumba ya kithajiri. Bai akiuka kwenda ts'i ya kure akagerwe cheo cha kukala muthawali, gonya auye kwao mudzini. 13 Bai kabila k'adzaandza charoche akiaiha ahumikie kumi na akiap'a kila mumwenga shilingi ya dhahabu,*19:13 Shilingi ya dhahabu ya siku zizo yere ina kima cha mishahara ya miezi mihahu. na akiambira, ‘H'aya hendani biashara na zizi p'esa muhaka nindihouya.’ 14 19:14 Mahe 4:17; 5:28; 13:44-46; 28:25-28Ela bai aryahu at'u ambao were akiuya ni adze aathawale were k'amamwendzi bule, kwa vizho makihuma at'u makaambe, ‘K'afumwendzi akale muthawali wehu yuyu.’
15 “Ela hatha hamwenga na vizho yuyu mut'u wahendwa akale muthawali na gonya akiuya mudzini. Bai arihouya waaiha aryahu ahumikie arioap'a ziryahu p'esa ili amanye kala mazihendera kazi na zikireha faidha yani. 16 Bai ye wa kwandza akidza na akiamba, ‘Bwana iryahu shilingi ya dhahabu yareha faidha ya shilingi nyingine kumi za dhahabu.’ 17 19:17-19 12:42-44; 16:10Bai ye mut'u ariyekwendahendywa muthawali akimwamba ye muhumiki, ‘Wahenda ut'u udzo sana, uwe u muhumiki mudzo! Bai kwa kukala wakala mwaminifu kwa maut'u mathithe, nindakuhendya ukale mubomu wa midzi kumi.’ 18 Gonya ye wa hiri akidza, akiamba, ‘Bwana iryahu shilingi yo ya dhahabu yareha faidha ya shilingi nyingine ts'ano za dhahabu.’ 19 Ye Bwanawe akimudzigidzya akimwamba, ‘Uwe nawe kala mubomu wa midzi mitsano.’ 20 Kula iye akidza mungine, akiamba, ‘Bwana, shilingiyo ya dhahabu siino, naihala nikiifunga kithambaani na nikiifitsa. 21 Kwani were unanigoshera mana uwe u mut'u mukali, unahendza kuhala vit'u virizho si zhako, kidza unahendza kuvuna ut'u uriwo k'uulayire.’ 22 Kusikira vizho ye bwanawe akimwamba, ‘Ii, muhumiki mui we! Nakupiga ulongo kwa ludhimilo mwenye. Uwe were unamanya kukala ni mwomu, nahendza kuhala vit'u virizho si zhangu, kidza nahendza kuvuna ut'u uriwo siulayire, 23 vikara kwanoni k'uihalire yo p'esa yangu na ukendayika bengi, ili nikiuya niyone idzazhala faidha?’ 24 Arihogonya kunena vizho akiagalukira o at'u mariokala maimire haho na akiambira, ‘Hedu muhokeni yo shilingi ya dhahabu na mumup'e ye ariye na kumi.’ 25 O at'u makimudzigidzya makiamba, ‘Aa, samba ye ana kumi kare Bwana?’ 26 19:26 Math' 25:29Ye Bwana akiamba, ‘Namwambira kamare, kila ariye nacho, andagerwa, ela iye ariye k'ana kit'u, andahokwa hatha cho aricho nacho. 27 Na vikara dzulu za aryahu mahadhui angu mariokala k'amendzi nikale muthawali wao, namala muarehe haha na mualage nalolera.’ ”
Jesu anangira Jerusalemu
Math'ayo 21:1-11; Mariko 11:1-11; Johana 12:12-19
28 Jesu arihogonya kunena vivi wathangulia kumbuka kwenda Jerusalemu. 29 Bai achenda hatha arihokala a hehi na kadzidzi ka Beth'ifage na ka Beth'ania kahi za Murima uifwao wa Mizeituni, akiahuma anafundzie airi mathangulie, 30 naye waahuma na malagizo gaga, “Endani hatha kadzidzi mudzikoerekezana nako. Muchangira mundaona mwana wa ndzowe ariye k'adzapandwa ni mut'u adzafungwa, muvuguleni mumurehe haha. 31 Mut'u yoyosi andiyemuza kwanoni munamuvugula, mwambeni, ‘Bwana ana haja naye.’ ” 32 Kwa vizho aryahu mariohumwa machenda na marihofika kila kit'u kikikala here arizhokala Jesu adzaambira. 33 Bai marihokala manamuvugula ye mwana wa ndzowe, hakimbola o enye makiamba, “Mbona munamuvugula ye mwana wa ndzowe kaheri?” 34 O anafundzi makidzigidzya makiamba, “Bwana ana haja naye.” 35 Bai makimureha kwa Jesu, makihala go maamba gao makigahandika ho dzulu za ye mwana wa ndzowe, gonya makimukweza Jesu ho dzulu. 36 19:36 2 Athaw 9:13Vikara arihokala akwendani, o at'u mahala maamba gao makigahandika mo ngirani. 37 Bai arihokala adzafika hehi mutharamuko wa Murima wa Mizeituni, ro kundi rosi ra anafundzie rikikwatya kumulika Mulungu kwa sauthi bomu kuno mana fwahiro kwa yo miujiza mariyokala mayona. 38 19:38 Zabu 118:26; Luk 2:14Makiamba,
“Naalikwe iye muthawali adzaye kahi za dzina ra Bwana!
Dheri narikale ko mulunguni,
na nguma yende kwa Mulungu ariye dzulu!”
39 Bai Mafarisayo angine kahi za ro kundi makimwamba Jesu vivi, “Mwalimu ambire o anafundzio manyamale hedu!” 40 19:40 Haba 2:11Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Ao makinyamala, be namwambira kamare gaga mawe gandapiga londzo kare.”
Jesu anarira kwa ut'u wa mudzi wa Jerusalemu
41 19:41-44 21:20-24Vikara Jesu arihofika hehi na wo mudzi, na arihoulola ho mbereze, warira kwa ut'u wa o at'ue. 42 19:42 Kumbu 32:29; Luk 2:14Akiamba, “Kala munamanya vivi rero virizho vingamurehera dheri! Ela bai k'amudima kuzhona, 43 kwa kukala siku zinadza, adhui enu mandamuzingira na kumuziza musipate hat'u ha kumbolera. 44 19:44 1:68; 21:6; Math' 24:2; Mar 13:2Nao mandamunonga-nonga kamare hamwenga na wo mudzi wenu. K'ahana iwe ririro k'arindapetulwa, kwa kukala k'amumanyire ho Mulungu arihomudzirira.”
Jesu anainga at'u kula kahi za ro Hekalu
Math'ayo 21:12-17; Mariko 11:15-19; Johana 2:13-22
45 Gonya Jesu achenda kahi za ro Hekalu na achendaainga o at'u osi mariokala managuza vit'u mumo. 46 19:46 Isa 56:7; Jere 7:11Akiambira, “Zhaorwa kukala, ‘Nyumba yangu indakala nyumba ya mavoyo,’ ela ninwi mudzaihendya p'anga ya kuzama aivi.” 47 19:47 21:37; 22:53; Math' 26:55; Mar 14:49; Joh 18:20Bada ya gago Jesu akiandza kufundisha kahi za ro Hekalu kila siku. Bai o alombi abomu, na alimu a Sheria na vilongozi a o at'u makikala manamala kumwalaga, 48 19:48 20:19; 22:2ela makikosa ngira ya kumwalagira, kwani o at'u osi were manamusirikizat'o sana hasiho kukirwa ni neno hatha mwenga.
