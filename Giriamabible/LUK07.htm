7
Jesu anahoza muhumiki wa mubomu wa jeshi
Math'ayo 8:5-13; Johana 4:43-54
1 7:1 Math' 7:28Bai Jesu arihogonya kunena maut'u gago arigokala anaambira at'u, wauka na achenda Kaperinaumu. 2 Vikara kuko Kaperinaumu kwere kuna mubomu mumwenga wa jeshi ra Kirumi, ariyekala na muhumiki amuhendzaye jeri-jeri. Bai muhumiki yuyu were ni mukongo hatha k'amanyikana kuthia. 3 K'uzhona zho yuyahu mubomu wa jeshi arihosikira uv̱oro wa Jesu, wahuma athumia a Kiyahudi mende makamwambe adze amuhoze ye muhumiki. 4 Bai marihofika kwa Jesu, mamusitira makiamba, “Mut'u yuyu ana hachi ya uwe umwavize, 5 mana anaahendza o at'u ehu na kidza nde ariyehuakira Sinagogi rehu.” 6 Kwa vizho Jesu achenda hamwenga nao. Ela arihofika hehi na yo nyumba, ye mubomu wa jeshi akihuma asenae makamwambe Jesu vivi, “Bwana, usidzibudzye bule, mana mimi siv̱irya kukala uwe udze wangire nyumbani mwangu, 7 ndosa sidzire kwako mimi mwenye. Ela nena neno na muhumiki wangu andahola. 8 Kwani mimi nami naikwa ts'ini za abomu angu, kidza nina shikari mario ts'ini zangu. Nikimwamba mumwenga, ‘Enda hat'u hasiku,’ anenda. Na yoyosi nindiyemwamba ‘Ndzo,’ anadza. Nikimwamba muhumiki wangu ‘Henda ut'u usiku,’ anauhenda.”
9 Jesu arihosikira maneno gago wangalazwa ni ye mut'u, na akigalukira ro kundi ra at'u mariokala manenda osi, akiamba, “Ninamwambira kukala sidzangwe kuona mut'u mwenye kuluhiro dza riri hatha kahi za ts'i ya Iziraeli.”
10 Bai aryahu at'u mario were madzahumwa mauya ko nyumbani na machendamuthekeza ye muhumiki k'ana ut'u.
Jesu anafufula muthana wa mwanamuche gungu
11 K'avitoere, Jesu achenda kahi za mudzi uifwao Naini, iye hamwenga na anafundzie na kundi bomu ra at'u. 12 7:12 1 Athaw 17:17Bai marihofika hehi na wo muv̱irya wa wo mudzi lola makithunganana na at'u mario were madzatsukula maithi. Mut'u yuyu were wazhalwa iye thu, na mameye were ni gungu. Bai kundi bomu ra at'u kula kahi za wo mudzi were ri hamwenga na yuyahu mwanamuche. 13 Bwana Jesu arihomona ye mameye ye mufwa, moyowe wamutika madzi na akimwamba, “Usirire m'ama.” 14 Gonya akigasongerera garyahu machera na akigagut'a na aryahu at'u mario were madzagatsukula makiima thii. Jesu akiamba, “We muthana hedu ungarara!” 15 7:15 1 Athaw 17:22-23Lola ye mufwa waungarara na akikeresi na akikwatya kunena, bai Jesu akimuhala na akimulazha kwa mameye.
16 7:16 1:68; 19:44; Nabii: Math' 16:14; 21:11, 46; 26:68; Mar 6:15; 8:28; 14:65; Luk 7:39; 9:8, 19; 22:64; 24:19; Joh 4:19; 6:14; 7:40; 9:17Kuona vizho o at'u osini mariokala haho madzalwa ni woga na makimulika Mulungu na kuamba, “Nabii mubomu adzafwambolera.” Kidza makiamba, “Mulungu adzire aokola at'ue.”
17 Bai uv̱oro uu dzulu za Jesu watsamuka kahi za ts'i yosi ya Judea, na hatha kungine-kungine.
Johana Mubat'izaji anaahuma anafundzie kwa Jesu
Math'ayo 11:1-6
18 Bai Johana achambirwa ni o anafundzie dzulu za maut'u gaga gosi. Ndo akiiha anafundzie airi 19 na akiahuma mende makamuze ye Bwana Jesu chamba nde iye aambwaye andakudza hedu seye matharize mungine. 20 Bai aryahu ahumwi marihofika kwa Jesu maamba, “Johana Mubat'izaji adzahuhuma hudze hukuze, chamba ndiwe ye mut'u aambwaye andakudza hedu siwe hutharize mungine?”
21 Na lola wakathi uwo wenye Jesu were anahoza at'u anji mariokala ni akongo manalumwa, na kidza wahoza anji mario were mana p'ep'o. Jesu vizho waahendya at'u anji mario were ni vipofu madime kuona. 22 7:22 4:18-19; Math' 11:5Kwa vizho akiadzigidzya o at'u mariokala madzahumwa ni Johana akiamba, “Uyani na mukamwambire Johana garyahu mudzigogaona na kugasikira, kwamba, vipofu manaona, virema manazhogat'o, at'u enye mahana manahozwa, enye masikiro ga makoto manasikira, nyufu zinafufulwa na Uv̱oro Udzo unaambirizwa at'u mario ni akiya. 23 Baha mut'u yuyahu ambaye mahendo gangu kwakwe k'agandakala kikwazo.”
Jesu ananena dzulu za Johana
Math'ayo 11:7-19
24 Bai bada ya aryahu at'u mario were madzahumwa ni Johana kuuka, Jesu wakwatya kunena na riryahu kundi ra at'u dzulu za ye Johana, akiamba, “Harya murihokwenda nyika, were mudzakwendalolani? Vidze were mudzakwendalola nyasi zifuswazo ni p'eho? 25 Bai ikikala si vizho vikara were mudzakwendalolani? Hedu were munalola mut'u adziyevwala nguwo mbidzo? Hatha bule! Mana at'u mavwalao nguwo za gali na kuishi maisha ga dzulu makala kahi za madzumba ga athawali. 26 Vikara nambirani were mudzakwendalolani? Were mudzakwendalola nabii? Ee, ni jeri, namwambira kamare iye adzakira nabii, iye wahumwa ni Mulungu adze anene dzulu za ye Masihi. 27 7:27 Mala 3:1; Kumbo 23:20; Mar 1:2; Luk 1:76Iye nde ariye Maoro gaorwa dzuluze kuamba, ‘Lola ninahuma mujumbe wangu mberezo, andiyekutengezera ngirayo kimbere.’ 28 Kunena wo ujeri, kahi za anadamu osi be k'akuna mut'u ariye ni mubomu kuriko Johana. Ela bai iye mut'u ariye nde muthithe kamare kahi za uthawali wa Mulungu be ni mubomu hatha kuriko iye Johana.”
29 7:29-30 Math' 21:327:29 3:7, 12“Lola o at'u osini na hatha o athozi a kodi mamusikira Johana. Na ao bai ni aryahu ambao makubali kukala yo ngira ya Mulungu ni jeri, kwa kukala were mabat'izwa ni Johana. 30 Ela o Mafarisayo na alimu a Sheria makahala mupango ario Mulungu waapangira na makikahala kubat'izwa ni Johana.”
31 Jesu akienderera kunena akiamba, “Ano at'u a siku zino hatha be ndaahalanisha na noni? Hatha madzahalana na noni ano? 32 At'u aa madzahalana na ahoho mario makeresi sokoni manazaziga. Kundi mwenga nikurambira ro ringine vivi, ‘Hudzamupigira kivoti, nanwi k'amuvinire; Hudzamuimbira wira wa hanga, nanwi k'amuririre.’ 33 7:33-34 Math' 9:14; Mar 2:18; Luk 5:337:33 1:15Mana Johana Mubat'izaji arihokudza, mut'u iye wakala k'arya hedu hatha kunwa divai, ela ko mukiamba, ‘Ana p'ep'o.’ 34 7:34 Math' 9:10Mwana wa Mudamu arihokudza na akikala anarya na kunwa, mukiamba, ‘Muloleni adzatona, kidza ni murevi, na anapatana na athozi a kodi na hatha at'u enye dambi.’ 35 Ela bai ulachu wa Mulungu unamanyikana kala ni wa jeri kukirira kwa osi mario manaukuluhira.”
Jesu anenda mudzini kwa Simoni Mufarisayo
36 7:36 11:37Vikara mut'u mumwenga ariye were ni Farisayo wamusunga Jesu ende akarye chakurya ko kwakwe nyumbani. Bai Jesu achenda na achendakeresi ho chakuryani. 37 7:37-38 Math' 26:6-7; Mar 14:3Bai lola kahi za mudzi uwo were kunatoa mwanamuche ariyekala maishage ni ga dambi. Vikara arihosikira kukala Jesu a kuko kwa ye Farisayo anarya chakurya, wauka na achenda na ts'azi iriyotengezwa kula kwa iwe ra alabasita ya mafuha ga marashi. 38 Arihofika kuko, akiima hehi na Jesu kuno anarira, bai go matsozige gakidweka mo maguluni mwa Jesu. Ye mwanamuche akigahangusa na zo nyereze, akigadonera go maguluge kunyesa ishima, gonya akigamwagira go marashi.
39 7:39 7:16Vikara yuyahu Farisayo ariyekala nde adzemusunga Jesu arihoona maut'u gago, akinena kimoyo-moyo akiamba, “Kala mut'u yuyu ni nabii jeri, akamanya kare, kala iye adziyemugut'a ni h'ani na kidza ni mut'u wa samupuli yani, akamanya kukala ni mudambi.” 40 Ndo Jesu akimwamba ye Farisayo akiamba, “Simoni, nina ut'u nimalao kukwambira.” Ye Farisayo akidzigidzya akiamba, “H'aya nambira mwalimu.” 41 Bai Jesu akiamba, “Kwereko at'u airi mario were manaiswa ni mut'u mumwenga ariyekala waakopesha. Mumwenga were anaiswa dinari*7:41 Dinari mwenga were ni p'esa iriyo na th'amani ya mariho ga siku ndzima. magana matsano na ye mungine ye were anaiswa dinari mirongo mitsano. 42 7:42 Math' 18:27Vikara osini airi makishindwa ni kumuriha, mana were k'amana kit'u. Bai yuyahu ariyekala anaaisa akiona baha aasamehe. H'aya unaona ni h'ani kahi za o airi ariye andamuhendza ye ariyeasamehe zaidhi?” 43 Ye Simoni akidzigidzya akiamba, “Naona ni yuyahu ariyesamehewa dheni bomu.” Jesu akimwamba, “U mujeri.” 44 7:44 Mwan 18:4; 1 T'imo 5:10Bai Jesu akigaluka akimulola ye mwanamuche na akimwamba ye Simoni, “Unamona yuno mwanamuche? Nidzihokudza mumu mwako nyumbani k'unangizirire madzi ga kuoga magulu,†7:44 Ho kapindi kahi za ts'i ya Iziraeli at'u were sana-sana menda na magulu. Kwa vizho makalaho were madzazhoga muda mure, na mangiraho kahi za nyumba ya mut'u were suthi maoge magulu kuusa ro vumbi na wo uchafu wa maguluni. ela yuno ye adzagalwanya magulu gangu na go matsozige gonya akagahangusa na zo nyereze. 45 Nidzihongira haha k'unikaribishire kwa kunidonera, ela yuno mwanamuche ye adzadonera magulu gangu hangu nidzihongirira haha. 46 7:46 Zabu 23:5Uwe k'unimwagirire mafuha ga kitswani, ela yuno ye adzagamwagira magulu gangu marashi. 47 Kwa ut'u uu bai, namala nikwambe vivi, gago mahendzo ga kwakwe mabomu, be vinanyesa kamare kukala zo dambize nyinji zidzasamehewa. Ela mut'u ariye adzasamehewa dambi ch'ach'e nde mwenye hendzo thithe.” 48 7:48 5:20; Math' 9:2; Mar 2:5Gonya Jesu akimwamba ye mwanamuche, “M'ama dambizo zidzasamehewa.” 49 K'uzhona zho aryahu ajeni angine mario were ma ho chakuryani makikwatya kuuzana-uzana na kuamba, “Ni h'ani yuno ariye anasamehe hatha dambi?”
50 7:50 Math' 9:22Na Jesu akimwamba ye mwanamuche, “Kuluhiro ra kwako ridzakwakola, h'aya enda na dheri.”
