15
Mashaka mafungahe ga mwisho
1 15:1 4:5; 15:7; 16:1; 17:1; 21:9Gonya nikiona ut'u ungine ubomu wa ajabu kidza wa kwangalaza. Naona malaika afungahe mariokala mana mapigo mafungahe ga mwisho. Mapigo gaga were ndo garigokamilisha k'oro za Mulungu. 2 15:2 4:6; 5:8; 13:15, 18; 14:2Gonya nikiona ut'u here bahari ya kilolo idziyotsanganywa na moho. Na haho k'anda ya bahari were haimire ao at'u mariokala madzarishinda riro rinyama, hamwenga na cho kizukache na iyo namba ya kwakwe.*15:2 Mana ya kushinda cho kizukache hamwenga na yo namba ya kwakwe, ni kukahala kukivoya cho kizuka na kukahala kwangizwa muv̱ano wa ro rinyama. At'u aa were madzagwirira vinubi marizhogerwa ni Mulungu. 3 15:3-4 4:1115:3 16:5-6; 19:2; 22:12; Kumbu 32:4; Zabu 145:17; Wira: Kumbo 15:1; Zabu 111:2; 139:14Na makiimba wira uriwoimbwa ni Musa muhumiki wa Mulungu, na wira wa iye Mwana Ng'ondzi. Wira uu were unaamba,
“H'ewe Bwana Mulungu mwenye nguvu zosi,
mahendogo ni madzo na ni mabomu sana!
Ngirazo ni za hachi na za jeri,
uwe u muthawali wa makabila gosi.†15:3 Maoro mangine ganaamba, ‘Uwe u Muthawali hatha kare na kare.’
4 15:4 14:7; Zabu 86:9; 98:2-3; Isa 2:2-3; Jere 10:6-7Ni h'ani Bwana asiyekugoha na kurilika dzinaro?
Uwe h'akeyo ndiwe uriye u mueri Bwana.
Na kwa kukala mahendogo ga hachi ganaonekana lwazu,
makabila gosi ga at'u gandakudza na gakuvoye uwe.”
5 15:5 11:19; Kumbo 40:34; Mahe 7:44Vinakira vivi, natsuha matso kuko mulunguni, nikiona ro hekalu ri lwazu na mumo ndani mwere muna ro hema ra kwanyesa kukala Mulungu anao. 6 15:6 1:13; Dani 10:5Na o malaika afungahe enye wadimi wa kureha mapigo mafungahe, machambola kahi za ro Hekalu makidza. Mere madzavala majoho maruhe ga kitani safi na madzadzifunga mikanda ya dhahabu mumo makathini mwao. 7 15:7 4:6; 14:10; 16:1Gonya mumwenga wa ao zhumbe ane, akiagolozera o malaika afungahe ndhuwa fungahe za dhahabu zidzizodzala k'oro za iye Mulungu aishiye kare na kare. 8 15:8 Kumbo 40:34-35; Isa 6:4; Izik 44:4Na wo mosi wa nguma na wadimi wa Mulungu ukiridzaza ro hekalu. Bai k'ahana mut'u ariyedima kwangira kahi za ro Hekalu muhaka garyahu mapigo mafungahe ga o malaika afungahe gakigoma.
