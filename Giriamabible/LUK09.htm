9
Jesu anaahuma anafundzie kumi na airi
Math'ayo 10:5-15; Mariko 6:7-13
1 9:1-6 10:1-12Badaye Jesu akiaiha aryahu anafundzi kumi na airi, na akiap'a nguvu na wadimi dzulu za p'ep'o osi na kuhoza makongo. 2 9:2 11:2Gonya akiahuma mende makahubiri Uv̱oro Udzo wa uthawali wa Mulungu na kuhoza akongo. 3 9:3 22:35Akiambira vivi, “Musitsukule chochosi mo charoni. Musitsukule mudhath'a, hedu mukoba, hedu leu, hedu p'esa, hedu k'andzu mbiri. 4 Nyumba yoyosi mundiyongira na mukaribishwet'o, kalani kahi za nyumba iyo hatha mukale munauka ndo mwambole. 5 9:5 Mahe 13:51Ela mwangiraho mudzini na o at'u masimukaribishe, bai haukeni na mukut'e-kut'e vumbi ra maguluni mwenu, kunyesa kukala mandamanya enye.”
6 Kwa vizho o anafundzi makiuka kwenda. Bai machenda kadzidzi kano na kadzidzi karya kuhubiri Uv̱oro Udzo na kuhoza akongo kila hat'u.
Kudangana kwa Herode
Math'ayo 14:1-12; Mariko 6:14-29
7 9:7-9 9:19Bai muthawali Herode arihosikira dzulu za gago gosi garigokala ganakala, wadangana, mana at'u angine mere manaamba, “Johana Mubat'izaji wafufuka.” 8 Angine o makiamba, “Nabii Elija adzatsumbuka,” na angine nao makiamba, “Mumwenga kahi za o manabii a kapindi adzafufuka.” 9 9:9 23:8Ela Herode akiamba, “Johana namutosa kitswa, vikara ni h'ani yuno niriye ninasikira uv̱oro uno dzuluze?” Bai akikala anathamani kumona.
Jesu anarisa at'u zaidhi ya alifu ts'ano
Math'ayo 14:13-21; Mariko 6:30-44; Johana 6:1-14
10 9:10-17 Math' 15:32-399:10 6:13; 10:17Vikara aryahu ahumwie marihouya, makudzamwambira Jesu go marigokala magahenda. Bai Jesu akiahala o anafundzie na makihenda nyeree, machenda kahi za mudzi uifwao Beth'isaida. 11 Ela ro kundi ra at'u rikivimanya vizho, naro rikimuthuwa. Bai Jesu akiakaribisha na akinena nao dzulu za wo uthawali wa Mulungu na kidza akiahoza ao mariokala mana haja ya kuhozwa.
12 Vikara dzuwa ririhokala rinenda kutswani aryahu anafundzie kumi na airi mamwendera na makimwamba, “Baha uafumule aa ili mende kahi za midzi na minda iriyo hehi na haha makadzimalire chakurya na ndazi, mana hano be ni nyika t'uhu.” 13 Jesu akiamba, “Ap'eni chakurya ninwi.” Ndo o anafundzie makimudzigidzya makimwamba, “Huna mikahe mitsano na makumba mairi thu labuda fukarigulire riri kundi rosi ra at'u chakurya.” 14 (Na haho were hana here at'u alifu ts'ano, alume.) Jesu akiambira o anafundzie akiamba, “Ambireni o at'u makeresi kahi za vithundhu zha here at'u mirongo mitsano-mitsano vivi.” 15 Bai o anafundzie makihenda vizho na o at'u makikeresi osi. 16 Jesu akihala yo mikahe mitsano na go makumba mairi, akilola dzulu mulunguni, akimboza muv̱era kwa Mulungu, gonya akivimogola-mogola na akiap'a o anafundzie maagazhire o at'u. 17 9:17 2 Athaw 4:43-44Nao osi makirya hatha makivilola. Marihogonya kurya o anafundzie makitsola-tsola zho visiku-visiku virizhokala vidzasala na makidzaza k'ahu kumi na mbiri.
P'et'ero ananena kukala Jesu ndiye Masihi
Math'ayo 16:13-19; Mariko 8:27-29
18 9:18 5:16Siku mwenga Jesu were anavoya h'akeye, bai o anafundzie makidza haho. Akiauza, akiamba, “Vidze o at'u manaamba mino ni h'ani?” 19 9:19 9:7-8; Luk 1:17; 7:16Makidzigidzya makiamba, “Angine manaamba uwe u Johana Mubat'izaji, angine o manaamba, u Elija na hatha angine manaamba u mumwenga wa o manabii a kapindi ariyefufuka.” 20 9:20 Math' 16:16; Luk 22:67Jesu akiauza akiamba, “H'aya nwi, munaamba mino ni h'ani?” P'et'ero akiamba, “Uwe ndiwe ye Masihi wa Mulungu.” 21 9:21 9:36Bai Jesu akiakokot'era ili masizhambire mut'u yoyosi.
Jesu anathala here andizhofwa
Math'ayo 16:21-28; Mariko 8:31–9:1
22 9:22 9:43-45; 17:25; 18:31-34; 24:6-7Jesu akienderera kunena akiamba, “Ye Mwana wa Mudamu suthi ateswe sana, na akahalwe ni athumia a Kiyahudi na alombi abomu na alimu a Sheria. Naye suthi alagwe, ela siku ya hahu andafufulwa.”
23 9:23-24 14:27; 17:33; Math' 10:38-39; Joh 12:25-26; 1 P'et 2:21Gonya akiambira osi vivi, “Mut'u akihendza kunithuwa mimi, suthi adzikane mwenye, adzihike musalabawe kila siku na anithuwe. 24 Mana kila amalaye kuthizha rohoye andayangamiza, ela kila andiyeyangamiza rohoye kwa ut'u wangu, nde andiyeithizha. 25 Kwani vindamufwahani mut'u akipata vit'u zhosi zha urumwengu gonya rohoye yangamike hedu adzanonge mwenye? 26 9:26 12:9; Math' 10:33; 2 T'imo 1:8; 2:12Kwani mut'u anioneraye haya hamwenga na gaga mafundisho gangu, bai ye Mwana wa Mudamu andamuonera haya siku iyo andiyokudza kahi za ngumaye mwenye na ya Baba na ya malaika aeri. 27 9:27 Math' 16:28Bai ninamwambira ujeri, haha hana at'u mario k'amandafwa ng'o hatha mawone wo uthawali wa Mulungu.”
Jesu anang'ala-ng'ala
Math'ayo 17:1-8; Mariko 9:2-13
28 9:28-29 5:16Siku nane bada ya Jesu kunena gaga, wamuhala P'et'ero, Johana na Jakobu, machendakwera dzulu za murima mavoye. 29 9:29-36 2 P'et 1:16-18Bai arihokala Jesu akuvoyani yo suraye yagaluka, zo nguwoze zikikala nyaruhe kidza za kung'ala-ng'ala zhomu. 30 Na lola haho henye hachambola at'u airi, Elija na Musa*9:30 Elija na Musa were ni vilongozi abomu a Kiyahudi ho kapindi. nao makinena naye. 31 9:31 9:22; 13:33Nao were manang'ala-ng'ala. Bai at'u ao were mananena na Jesu dzulu za viryahu arizho were andathimiza na kifwache kiricho were kindakala ko Jerusalemu 32 9:32 Joh 1:14wakathi uwo P'et'ero na andziye were marere uts'indzizi wa fungo ela bai marihong'alwa ni matso, mawona wo mulangazawe hamwenga na aryahu at'u airi mariokala maimire naye. 33 Vikara Elija na Musa marihokala manauka, P'et'ero wamwamba Jesu, “Mwalimu ni vidzo zho zha fu haha! Nahuake vidzumba vihahu, kimwenga chako, kimwenga cha Musa na kimwenga cha Elija.” P'et'ero were tha k'amanya ananenani.
34 Naye tha were k'adzatosa kukidza ingu rikidzaafinikira, na o anafundzi makigwirwa ni woga marihokala manafinikirwa ni ro ingu. 35 9:35 3:22; Kumbu 18:15Mo inguni muchambola mumiro wa mut'u ukiamba, “Yuyu ni Mwanangu niriyemutsagula, musirikizeni iye.” 36 9:36 9:21Bada ya yo sauthi kugonya kunena, o anafundzie mamona Jesu a h'akeye renye. Bai o anafundzie k'amazhambirire mut'u vivi wakathi uwo marihokala madzazhona hatha mathuwa kukala nazho enye.
Jesu anamboza muvulana p'ep'o
Math'ayo 17:14-18; Mariko 9:14-27
37 Siku ya hiriye, marihokala madzatharamuka kula ko murimani, ngororoki bomu ya at'u yakudzathunganana na Jesu. 38 Kahi za yo ngororoki hachambola mwanamulume mumwenga ariyenena zhomu zhenye akiamba, “Mwalimu, nakuvoya umulole yuyu muthana wangu, mana sina mwana mungine. 39 Lola ana p'ep'o ariye nikumugwaga na akaandza kupiga k'ululu akamuhendya afitike na ambole fulo ra kanwani. K'avimwendera kure kamare, hatha vinamwanonga bai. 40 Nidzaasitira o anafundzio mamwamboze ye p'ep'o ela k'amazhadimire.”
41 Jesu akidzigidzya akiamba, “H'enwi at'u a kizhazi cha kwangamika ngira, musio na kuluhiro, nindakala nanwi hatha rini? Hedu nindamuvumirira hatha rini? Murehe kuno ye muthanao.” 42 Vikara ye muvulana arihokala anadza ye p'ep'o akimupiga chavu na kufitika, ela Jesu akimuchemera akimwamboza ye p'ep'o, ye muvulana akihola, akimuhala akimulazha kwa babaye. 43 9:43-45 9:21-22Na lola o at'u osi makingalazwa kamare ni zo nguvu za Mulungu.
Jesu anathala kaheri dzulu za kifwache
Math'ayo 17:22-23; Mariko 9:30-32
Bai marihokala o at'u machere kungalala-ngalala na go arigokala Jesu adzagahenda, iye Jesu waambira o anafundzie akiamba, 44 “Sirikizani sana gaga nimalago kumwambira. Kwani ye Mwana wa Mudamu andalazhwa mikononi mwa at'u.” 45 9:45 18:34Ela o anafundzie k'amang'alirirwe ni gago arigokala Jesu anaambira. Kwani kwao, were vidzafitswa ili masidime kuvielewa, na kidza ao were ni aoga na kumuza dzulu za gago.
Jesu ananena dzulu za ubomu
Math'ayo 18:1-5; Mariko 9:33-37
46 9:46-48 22:24-26Bai o anafundzie makinenezana dzulu za mubomu ni h'ani kahi zao. 47 Ela Jesu akigamanya kare go maazo gao, kwa vizho akihala muhoho muthithe akimurungaraza ho hehi naye. 48 9:48 10:16; 22:26; Math' 10:40; 20:26-27; Mar 10:43-44; Joh 13:20Gonya akiambira, “Kila amuhokeraye muhoho muthithe dza yuyu kwa dzina rangu, ananihokera mimi, na ye anihokeraye mimi k'anihokera mimi bule, ela anamuhokera iye ariyenihuma, mana ye ariye ni muthithe kahi zenu nyosi be nde mubomu.”
Ariye k'akinyume chenu a upande wenu
Mariko 9:38-40
49 Johana achambola kunena akiamba, “Bwana, fwaona mut'u anamboza p'ep'o kwa dzinaro, naswi hukimukahaza, kwa kukala si mumwenga wehu.” 50 9:50 11:23; Math' 12:30Jesu akimwamba, “Musimukahaze, mana mut'u ariye k'akinyume chenu, a upande wenu.”
Kadzidzi ka Asamaria kanamukahala Jesu
51 9:51 24:50-51; Math' 19:1; Mar 10:1; Mahe 1:9Vikara siku za Jesu kuhalwa kwenda mulunguni zirihofika hehi, Jesu wakat'a shauri kwenda Jerusalemu. 52 9:52 10:33Bai akihuma at'u mamuthangulie nao machenda kahi za kadzidzi ka Asamaria ili mamuthayarishire hat'u. 53 Ela o at'u a kuko makikahala kumukaribisha Jesu kwa kukala were anenda Jerusalemu.
54 9:54 2 Athaw 1:10, 12K'uzhona anafundzie airi, Jakobu na Johana marihoona vizho makiamba, “Bwana, unahendza fuihe moho kula mulunguni udze uanonge-nonge ano [dza here Elija arizhohenda]?” 55 Ela Jesu akihunduka na akiadumulira, [akiamba, “Ninwi k'amumanya ni roho ya samupuli yani yo muriyo nayo. Mana Mwana wa Mudamu k'adzire kudzangamiza maisha ga at'u, ela wakudza kugaokola.”] 56 Bai makiuka na machenda kahi za kadzidzi kangine.
Garama za kumuthuwa Jesu
Math'ayo 8:19-22
57 Vikara Jesu na anafundzie marihokala ma mo ngirani, makwendani, mut'u mumwenga wamwamba Jesu, “Mut'u mimi nindakuthuwa kila undikokwenda.” 58 Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Makala gana p'anga, na unyama wa kubururuka una vitsunji zhao, ela Mwana wa Mudamu, k'ana hat'u ha kuika mbavu.”
59 Badaye Jesu akimwamba mut'u mungine, “Nithuwa.” Ela ye mut'u akidzigidzya akiamba, “Bwana niricha nikamuzike baba kwandza be nidze nikuthuwe.” 60 Jesu akimwamba, “Ariche at'u mariofwa mazike nyufu zao, ela uwe enda ukauhubiri wo uthawali wa Mulungu.” 61 9:61 1 Athaw 19:20Mut'u mungine ye akimwamba Jesu, “Mimi nindakuthuwa Bwana, ela niricha kwandza nikaalage adamu angu ko nyumbani.”
62 9:62 11:2; Afil 3:13Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Mut'u yoyosi agwiraye jembe kurima gonya akalola nyuma k'afwaha kuhenda kazi kahi za uthawali wa Mulungu.”
