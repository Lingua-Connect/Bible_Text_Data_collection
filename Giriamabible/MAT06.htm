6
Jesu anakanya dzulu za kuhendza kuonewa
1 6:1 23:5Jesu akienderera kufundisha akiamba, “Ukihenda ut'u udzo, usinyese at'u kukala udzahenda ut'u udzo, kwani ukidzanyesa Babayo wa mulunguni k'andakunosa chochosi. 2 Usendemwaviza mukiya, gonya ukapiga gunda, dza vizho at'u a kudzihendya mahendazho mumo ngira-ngira na masinagogini. Vizho zhosi manavihenda kwa manamala kulikwa, nami namwambira ujeri wenye kukala manoso gao gosi magonya kare kugapata. 3 Ela uchendamup'a kit'u mut'u mukiya be hatha wo mukonowo wa kumots'o kidza usimanye zho mukonowo wa kurya udzizhohenda. 4 Naye Babayo aonaye ga sirini andakunosa.”
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kuvoya
Luka 11:1-4
5 “Muvoyaho, musikale here at'u a kudzihendya mahendazho. Kazi yao ni kuima mathanoni hedu masinagogini ili maonewe ni at'u. Bai ujeri wenye at'u dza ao magonya kare kupata mariho gao. 6 Ela bai, uvoyaho, ngira nyumbani mwako ufunge muryango na umuvoye Babayo ariye sirini, naye kwa kukala anaona ga sirini, andakup'a zawadiyo. 7 Muvoyaho musihaye-haye bule, here at'u masiomumanya Mulungu mahendazho. Kwani o manaona makinena maneno manji ndo Mulungu andaasikira. 8 6:8 6:32; Luk 12:30Hatha, musihende vizho bule. Kwani ye Babiyenu be anamanya zho mumalazho hatha kabila k'amudzavoya. 9 6:9 Izik 36:23Bai muvoyaho, voyani vivi:
Babiyehu uriye ko mulunguni,
dzinaro narikale rieri,
10 6:10 4:17; 7:21; 12:50; 18:14; 21:31; 26:39-42; Mar 3:35; 14:36; Luk 22:42; Joh 4:34; 6:38-40; Mahe 21:14Uthawaliwo naudze.
Mahendzogo nagahendeke kuku ts'i,
here gahendekazho ko mulunguni.
11 6:11 6:24-34Hup'e vivi rero chakurya chehu cha kuhuthosha.
12 6:12 6:15Na uhusamehe makosa gehu,
here zho huasamehazho ao mahukoserao.
13 6:13 10:16-25; 24:3-28; Jako 1:12-15; Joh 17:15; 2 Ath'es 3:3; 2 T'imo 4:18Na usihuhirike majezoni,
ela huokole na ye Mui.
14 6:14-15 5:24; Luk 6:37; Aefe 4:32“Kwani mukiasamehe at'u mamukosao, na Babiyenu wa mulunguni naye andamusamehe makosa genu. 15 6:15 18:35Ela kala o at'u mamukoserao k'amundaasamehe, ye Babiyenu wa mulunguni naye k'andamusamehe bule.”
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kufunga na maut'u mangine-mangine
16 6:16-17 Isa 58:5; Math' 4:2; 9:14-15; 17:21; Mar 2:18; 9:29; Luk 2:37; 4:2; 5:33; 18:12; Mahe 13:2-3; 14:23; 2 Akor 6:5; 11:27“Mufungaho musinyese kukala k'amuna furaha dza zho at'u a kudzihendya mahendazho. Kwani o be ni kukundza uso, ili mamanywe kukala madzafunga. Bai namwambira jeri wenye kukala ao mahokera manoso gao kare. 17 Ela ufungaho, oga uso, gonya udzihake mafuha, 18 ili at'u masimanye kukala udzafunga, isihokala Babayo ariye sirini. Bai kwa kukala iye anaona kahi za siri andakup'a zawadi.”
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kuika akiba mulunguni
Luka 12:32-34
19 6:19 Jako 5:2-3“Musiike akiba zenu kahi za urumwengu uu, zikariwa ni p'oho, na zikagwira k'anga, hedu aivi makadzaziiya. 20 6:20 19:21; Mar 10:21; Luk 12:33Hatha bule, ikani akiba zenu mulunguni. Mana kuko k'akuna p'oho a kuzirya, hedu k'anga ya kuzononga-nonga. Kidza k'akuna aivi madimao kubanda na kuziiya. 21 Kwani haho handihokala akibayo, ndo handihokala moyowo.”
22 Bai Jesu waenderera kufundisha akiamba, “Matso ndo taa ra mwiri. Kwa vizho kala matsogo ni madzo, mwiriwo wosi nikudzala mulangaza. 23 Ela kala matsogo ni makongo, mwiriwo wosi nikwadzala kiza. Bai kala wo mulangaza ndani yenu udzagaluka kiza, kiza kicho be hatha k'akina mwandziwe bule. 24 6:24-34 6:11K'akuna adimaye kuahendera kazi mathajiri airi. Kwani akihenda vizho, andamuhendza mumwenga ela ye mungine andamumena. Mumwenga andamuhendera kaziye t'ot'ot'o, ela ye mungine andamuv̱udhya. K'amudima kumuhumikira Mulungu na kuno munahumikira mali.”
Jesu anaambira anafundzie masihende wasiwasi bule
Luka 12:22-31
25 6:25 Afil 4:6; 1 P'et 5:7“Kwa ut'u uwo wenye bai, ninamwambira kukala musihende wasiwasi dzulu za maisha genu na miri yenu, kala mundaryani, hedu mundanwani, hedu mundavalani. Samba uzima udzakira chakurya, na mwiri nawo udzakira nguwo? 26 6:26 10:29-31; Luk 12:6-7Munawona wo unyama wa kubururuka? K'aurima, na k'auvuna ukangiza lutsagani bule, kidza Babiyenu wa mulunguni anaurisa. H'e seze ninwi murio muna kima kibomu kuukira uwo? 27 Ni h'ani kahi zenu adimaye kuhenda wasiwasi hatha akangeza ngaa saa mwenga kahi za maishage? 28 Na ni kwanoni muna wasiwasi dzulu za nguwo? Hedu lolani go maluwa ga vuweni zho gamerazho. K'agahenda kazi, hedu hatha kudzifumira nguwo. 29 6:29 1 Athaw 10Ela ninamwambira kukala, hatha Selemani na ushahawe wosi, kidza k'avwaliret'o dza gago maluwa. 30 6:30-31 14:31H'enwi at'u a kuluhiro thithe nwi! Kala Mulungu anadima kuzipamba zo nyasi za vuweni ambazo rero zi haha na machero zinatsufwa mohoni zishe, h'e seze nwi. Samba Mulungu andamuvika zaidhi? 31 Kwa vizho bai, musithapethape kuamba mundaryani, mundanwani, hedu mundavalani, 32 6:32 6:8kwani gago gosi ndo mamalago at'u masiomumanya Mulungu. Ela ninwi musithapethape, kwani Babiyenu wa mulunguni anamanya kare kukala maut'u gago gosi munagamala naye andamup'a. 33 6:33 1 Athaw 3:11-14; Zabu 37:4-5Ela umaleni uthawali wa kwakwe hamwenga na uhachiwe kwandza, na zho vingine zhosi mundangezezwa. 34 6:34 Kumbo 16:4, 19-20Bai kwa ut'u uwo wenye, musihende wasiwasi dzulu za maut'u ga machero, kwa kukala siku iyo ya machero be indadzihendera wasiwasi yenye. Kwani kila siku ina thabuze za kuthosha.”
