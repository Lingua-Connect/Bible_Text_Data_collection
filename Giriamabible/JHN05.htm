5
Jesu anahoza mwanamulume birikani
1 Badaye Jesu wambuka Jerusalemu kwa sikuk'uu ya Kiyahudi. 2 Vikara kuko Jerusalemu hehi na Muv̱irya wa Ng'ondzi kuna birika ambaro kwa Kieburania rinaifwa Betizath'a. Birika riri were rina ukuta uriwo na miryango mitsano. 3 Bai kahi za birika riri were hana akongo anji sana mahembelele, masoona, vidende, na akuholoza. [ 4 Nao were manathariza madzi gatigwe, mana were wakathi kwa wakathi, malaika wa Bwana anatharamuka mo birikani na kugatiga go madzi. Vikara mut'u wa kwandza wa kwangira mo birikani bada ya madzi kutigwa wahozwa ukongowe ariwokala nawo.] 5 Bai haho were hana mut'u mumwenga ariyekala adzafisha miaka mirongo mihahu na minane analumwa thu. 6 Jesu arihomona na kumanya kukala adzakala haho kwa muda mure, wamuza akiamba, “Vidze unamala kuhola?”
7 Ye munyonge akiamba, “Bwana mut'u mimi, sina mut'u wa kunangiza birikani madzi gatigwaho, mana nikijeza kwangira thu, nakatw'a ni mut'u mungine.” 8 5:8-9 Math' 9:6-7Jesu akimwamba, “Uka, uhale godhororo wanende.” 9 5:9 5:16, 18; 7:21-22; 9:14; Mar 2:27-28Haho henye ye mut'u akihola. Akihala godhoro ra kwakwe akinenda.
Vinakala vivi were ni siku ya Sabato. 10 5:10 Kumbo 20:8-10; Jere 17:21-22Bai o Ayahudi makimwamba ye ariyekala adzahozwa, “Rero ni Sabato na kulingana na sheria yehu k'uv̱irya kutsukula godhoro bule.”
11 Iye mut'u akiamba, “Ye adziyenihoza adzanambira, ‘Hala godhororo wende.’ ”
12 Bai makimuza makiamba, “Ni h'ani mut'u yuno adziyekwambira uhale godhororo na wende kwenu?”
13 5:13 6:15; 11:54; 12:36; Math' 8:18; 13:36Ela ye mut'u ariyehozwa k'amumanyire ye ariyekala adzamuhoza, kwa kukala hat'u haho were hana kundi ra at'u na Jesu were adzahenyeza akenda.
14 5:14 Math' 9:2; Jako 5:16Badaye Jesu akimona kahi za ro Hekalu akimwamba, “Lola u muzima vikara. Usihende dambi kaheri, p'ore ukagwirwa ni ut'u uwi zaidhi kukira uu.”
15 5:15 9:11Ndo ye mut'u achenda na akiambira o Ayahudi kukala ni Jesu nde ariyemuhoza.
Jesu ana wadimi wa Mulungu
16 Kwa ut'u uu o Ayahudi makiandza kumutesa Jesu, kwa kukala were adzahenda kihendo kiki siku ya Sabato. 17 Jesu akiadzigidzya, akiamba, “Baba anahenda kazi kila wakathi, na mimi nami nav̱irya nihende kazi.”
18 5:18 Kwalaga: Math' 14:5; Joh 7:1, 25; 8:37, 40; 11:53; Baba na Mwana: 1:34; 3:35; 8:16-19; 10:17-18, 29-38; 14:8-11, 20, 28; 15:9-10; 16:27-28; 17Neno riri raahendya o Ayahudi mazidhi kumala kumwalaga Jesu, kwa kukala were k'avundzire Sabato thu, ela were adzaamba kukala iye Mulungu be ni babaye mwenye, na kwa vivi wadzihendya kukala yu karakara na Mulungu.
19 5:19 1:34Ndo Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Ninamwambira ujeri, Mwana k'adima kuhenda kit'u chochosi h'akeye. Mut'u iye anahenda go aonago Babaye anagahenda. Go ahendago Baba ndo ahendago Mwana. 20 5:20 3:35Kwani Baba anamuhendza Mwanawe na anamwanyesa kila kit'u ambacho ye mwenye anakihenda. Andamwanyesa ye Mwanawe maut'u mabomu zaidhi ga kuhenda, nanwi mundangalala. 21 5:21 Arum 4:17; Aefe 2:5; Joh 11:25Here Baba afufulazho nyufu na kuzip'a uzima, Mwana naye dzazho ni kuap'a uzima aryahu amalao kuap'a uzima. 22 5:22 3:18; 5:27, 30; 9:39; 12:47-48; Mahe 10:42; 1 Joh 2:28Ye Baba k'amuhukumu mut'u yoyosi, ela wamup'a Mwanawe wadimi kamili wa kuhukumu, 23 5:23 Afil 2:10-11kwamba osi mamuheshimu Mwana here mamuheshimuzho ye Baba ariyemuhuma. 24 5:24 3:16; 5:22; 6:35; 8:51; 10:28; 11:25-26; 14:6; 17:2; 20:31; 1 Joh 2:25; 3:14Ninamwambira ujeri, mut'u yoyosi asikiraye maneno gangu na kumukuluhira ye ariyenihuma, yuna uzima wa kare na kare. K'andahukumiwa, ela adzagonya kare kukira kifwani hatha uzimani. 25 5:25 11:43; Mar 5:41; Luk 7:14Ninamwambira ujeri, wakathi unadza na udzire kare, ambaho nyufu zindasikira sauthi ya Mwana wa Mulungu na ao mandioisikira mandaishi. 26 5:26 1:4Kwani here arizho Baba mwenye nde chaho cha uzima, dza vizho zhenye Mwanawe adzamuhendya chaho cha uzima. 27 Na adzamup'a Mwana wadimi wa kuhukumu, kwa kukala iye ni Mwana wa Mudamu.
28 “Musangalale kwa gaga, mana wakathi unadza ambawo osi mario mbirani mandasikira sauthiye, 29 5:29 11:24na mandambola mo mbirani. Na ao mariohenda madzo mandafufuka na kukala moyo na osi mariohenda mai mandafufuka na mandahukumiwa.”
Jesu anaenderera kunena na Ayahudi
30 5:30 4:34“Mimi h'akiyangu sidima kuhenda kit'u chochosi. Mut'u mimi ninahukumu thu here Mulungu anambirazho, kwa vizho hukumu yangu ni ya ujeri, mana sihenda go nihendzago mimi, ela nahenda garyahu gamalwago ni iye ariyenihuma.
31 5:31-32 5:36“Nikimboza ushahidhi dzulu yangu mwenye go ninenago si ga ujeri. 32 Ela kuna mungine anambozeraye ushahidhi hat'u hangu na ninamanya uwo ushahidhiwe dzulu yangu ni jeri.
33 5:33-34 1:15, 19, 34“Mwahirika at'u kwa Johana, na akinena ujeri dzulu zangu. 34 Si kwa kukala nidzathumainia ushahidhi niupatao ula kwa mudamu, ela ninanena vivi kwamba mukuluhire. 35 Iye were ni taa riakaro na kung'ala, nanwi mukihendza kufurahi kwa muda kahi za mulangazawe.
36 5:36 10:25, 38; 14:10-11; 15:24, 27“Ela wo ushahidhi niriwo nawo ni mubomu kuriko wa Johana, nawo ni zizo kazi arizonip'a Baba nizithimize. Bai kazi zizi zenye nizihendazo ndo zirizo zinanambozera ushahidhi kukala Baba wanihuma. 37 5:37 1:34; Kumbo 20:19; Kumbu 4:12Na ye Baba mwenye ariyenihuma anamboza ushahidhi dzulu zangu. Ela ninwi k'amudzamusikira sauthiye hedu hatha kumona umbore, 38 5:38 16:9na kidza nenore k'amunaro ndani mwenu, kwani k'amumukuluhira iye ariyemuhuma. 39 5:39 Luk 24:27, 44; Mahe 13:27; 17:11; 2 T'imo 3:15-16; 1 P'et 1:10-11Munashoma Maoro kwa chadi mukiona kukala kwa gago mundapata uzima wa kare na kare. Gaga ndo Maoro ganilazhirago ushahidhi, 40 ela kidza munakahala kudza kwangu kupata uzima.
41 5:41 7:18“Simala ishima kula kwa at'u. 42 Ela ninamanya kukala k'amuna mahendzo ga Mulungu mioyoni mwenu. 43 Mut'u mimi nakudza kahi za dzina ra Baba, ela k'amunihokerere, mut'u mungine akidza kwa dzinare mundamuhokera. 44 5:44 5:41Munadimadze kukuluhira ikidzakala mwakubali kudzip'a nguma enye kwa enye na k'amuhenda chadi chochosi kupata nguma imbolayo kwa Mulungu h'akeye? 45 5:45 Kumbu 31:24-29Hatha vizho musione kukala nindamushithaki kwa Baba, Musa nde andiyemushithaki ninwi. Naye ni yuyahu Musa ambaye mudzamutumainia. 46 5:46 Kumbu 18:15, 18; Luk 24:27; Joh 6:14; Mahe 3:22; 7:37Kala jeri mwamukuluhira Musa ngere mwanikuluhira, mana iye wandhika dzulu zangu. 47 5:47 Luk 16:29-31Ela chamba k'amukuluhira zho arizhondhika Musa, munadimadze kukuluhira go ninenago?”
