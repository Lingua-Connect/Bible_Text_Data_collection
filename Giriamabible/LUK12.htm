12
Ts'ats'u ya Mafarisayo
1 12:1 Math' 16:6; Mar 8:15Vikara wakathi uwo maalifu na maalifu ga at'u gadzazana hatha vikikala manakwalana-kwalana kamare. Bai Jesu akikwatya kunena na o anafundzie kwandza akiamba, “Dzimanyirireni na yo ts'ats'u ya Mafarisayo ambayo ni ko kudzihendya kwao. 2 12:2 8:17; Mar 4:22Kila kit'u kidzichobiningizwa kindagunulwa, na kila kit'u kidzichofitswa kindaonewa. 3 Kwa vizho bai gago murigonena kizani, gandasikirwa mulangazani, na zo njama murizonena kahi za nyumba zidzizofungwa miryango, zindatangazwa zhomu zhenye.”*12:3 Haha go maneno genye ga Kiyunani ganaamba, ‘zindatangazwa dzulu za madari.’ Ayahudi were makakeresi dzulu za nyumba zao makasumurira.
Jesu anaafundisha anafundzie mamwagohe Mulungu h'akeye
Math'ayo 10:28-31
4 “Namwambira asenangu, musogohe ao malagao mwiri, na bada ya kuhenda gago, k'amadima kumuhenda vingine. 5 12:5 Aebu 10:31Ela bai nindamwanyesa iye ariye ni mumwagohe: Ninwi mwagoheni iye ambaye bada ya kuwalaga wo mwiri ana wadimi wa kuuhala na akautsuha Jehanamu. Ee, namwambira kamare, mwagoheni iye!
6 “Vidze samba utsongo utsano unaguzwa rahisi renye,†12:6 Haha go maneno genye ga Kiyunani ganaamba, ‘unaguzwa mapeni mairi.’ kidza ko k'ahana hatha kamwenga kariko Mulungu anadima kukasahau. 7 12:7 21:18H'aya zho mbai, hatha zo nyere zenu kidza Mulungu anazimanya isabuye. Kwa vizho bai musogohe bule, mana ninwi muna th'amani kukira iho ra utsongo!”
Kumukubali na kumukana Jesu
Math'ayo 10:32-33; 12:32; 10:19-20
8 Jesu akienderera kunena na akiamba, “Namwambira kamare, kila mut'u andiyenikubali mbere za at'u, iye Mwana wa Mudamu naye andamukubali mbere za malaika a Mulungu. 9 12:9 9:26; Mar 8:38Ela kila mut'u andiyenikahala mbere za at'u, iye naye andakahalwa mbere za malaika a Mulungu. 10 Na kila mut'u andiyenena rorosi kinyume cha ye Mwana wa Mudamu, andasamehewa, ela mut'u yoyosi andiyenena kufuru dzulu za Roho Mweri, k'andasamehewa bule. 11 12:11-12 21:14-15; Math' 10:19-20; Mar 13:11Mukihirikwa masinagogini kwendashithakiwa, hedu mukihirikwa mbere za athawali na maluwali, be hatha musihende wasiwasi wa kuamba mundadzigidzyani hedu mundanenani, 12 kwa kukala ye Roho Mweri andamufundisha saa iyo-iyo gago muv̱iryago kunena.”
Fumbo ra shaha muzuzu
13 Bai hachambola mut'u mumo kundini akimwamba Jesu, “Mwalimu, mwambire ndugu yangu anigazhire wo ufwa nami!” 14 Jesu akimudzigidzya akimwamba, “Musena, h'aya ni h'ani ariyenihendya mulamuli hedu mugazhi wenu wa ufwa?” 15 12:15 1 T'imo 6:9-10Gonya akiambira osini akiamba, “Kalani matso, na mudzimanyirire na kila samupuli ya th'amaa ya kuhendza vit'u, kwa kukala wo uzima wa mut'u be k'aukahi za wo unji wa vit'u arizho nazho bule.”
16 Gonya Jesu akiambozera fumbo riri, akiamba, “Kwereko thajiri mumwenga ariye mundawe wazhalat'o sana. 17 K'uzhona akidziuza kimoyo-moyo na kuamba, ‘Vino be hatha ndahendadze mut'u mino? Mana be sina hat'u ha kuika mavuno gangu gosi.’ 18 Gonya akiamba, ‘Nindizhohenda ni vivi, nindazivomola ziryahu ts'aga zangu na niake nyingine bomu zaidhi na bai nindawika wo mutsere wangu wosi na zho vit'u zhangu mumo.’ 19 12:19-20 Zabu 49:20Nikigonya vizho, h'aya nidziambe, ‘Mut'u uwe be una maut'u manji madzo-madzo gandigokuhala miaka minji kugoma, vikara thuwa kuhurira urye, unwe na ufwahirwe.’
20 “Ela Mulungu akimwamba, ‘Ii muzuzu we! Usiku wa rero be rohoro, rindamalwa, h'aya zho vit'uzho urizho udzadziikira vindakala zha h'ani?’ ” 21 Bai Jesu akimarigiza na kuamba, “H'aya, vivi be ndizho vindizhokala kwa mut'u yoyosi adziikiraye vit'u kuno ko si shaha kahi za maut'u ga Mulungu.”
Musihende wasiwasi
Math'ayo 6:25-34
22 12:22-32 11:3Gonya Jesu akiambira o anafundzie akiamba, “Kwa ut'u uwo wenye bai, ninamwambira kukala, musihende wasiwasi dzulu za maisha genu, na miri yenu, kala mundaryani, hedu mundavalani. 23 Mana uzima udzakira chakurya, na mwiri nawo udzakira nguwo. 24 12:24 Zabu 147:9Hedu lolani go makunguru. K'agarima na k'agavuna, k'agana hat'u ha kuika vit'u, hedu hatha ts'aga, ela Mulungu anagarisa. Nanwi, samba muna kima k'ana nyinji hatha kukira wo unyama! 25 Ni h'ani kahi zenu adimaye kuhenda wasiwasi hatha akangeza ngaa saa kahi za maishage? 26 Bai ikikala ut'u uthithe wenye dza uu k'amudima kuuhenda, kwanoni muhende wasiwasi na go mangine?
27 12:27 1 Athaw 10“Hedu lolani zho maluwa gamerazho, k'agahenda kazi, hedu hatha kudzifumira nguwo. Ela namwambira kamare, hatha Selemani na ushahawe wosi, be k'avwaliret'o dza gago maluwa. 28 12:28 Math' 14:31H'enwi at'u a kuluhiro thithe nwi! Kala Mulungu anadima kuzipamba zo nyasi za vuweni ambazo rero zi haha na machero zinatsufwa mohoni zishe, h'e seze nwi. Samba Mulungu andamuvika zaidhi? 29 Bai musithapethape kumala mundaryani hedu mundanwani, musihende wasiwasi dzulu za maut'u gago. 30 Mana gago gosi ndo at'u a urumwengu mamalago, naye Babiyenu anamanya kukala maut'u gago munagamala. 31 Ela umaleni wo uthawaliwe kwandza, na zho vingine mundangezezwa.”
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kuika ushaha mulunguni
Math'ayo 6:19-21
32 12:32 Isa 41:14; Luk 22:29“Musogohe ninwi badhi thithe, kwa kukala Babiyenu adzaona ni vidzo kumup'a wo uthawali. 33 12:33 Math' 6:19, 20; Luk 11:41; 18:22; Mahe 2:45; 4:34-37; Akol 3:1-2Kwa vizho bai guzani zho vit'u murizho nazho, na zo p'esa muap'e akiya. Nanwi kwa kuhenda vizho, mundakala mudzadziikira akiba wo ushaha wenu ko mulunguni ambako be k'aundagoma, mana k'akuna mwivi adimaye kuusongerera hehi, na k'akuna p'oho madimao kudzawanonga. 34 Mana haho hariho zo akiba zenu be ndo handihokala yo mioyo yenu.”
Ahumiki adzo na ai
Math'ayo 24:45-51
35 12:35-36 Math' 25:1Jesu akienderera kunena na akiamba, “Dzifungeni nguwo, na mukale thayari-thayari, kuno mataa genu ganaaka. 36 12:36 Ugun 3:20Kalani here at'u mario manamuthariza bwana wao auye kula harusini, ili andihouya na kubisha, mamuvugulire kare. 37 Bai baha ao ahumiki ambao ye bwana andakudzaathekeza mathayari-thayari. Namwambira ujeri, iye andavwala nguwo za kazi na aahumikire. Iye andaambira makeresi na aambozeze chakurya. 38 Kidza baha ao ahumiki ambao ye bwana wao hatha adzaho usiku wa manane hedu jogolo ra kwandza andakudzaathekeza mathayari-thayari. 39 12:39 Math' 24:43Ela bai manyani vivi, kalaho mwenye nyumba anadima kumanya zo saa andizokudzirira mwivi, be k'angakubalire nyumbaye ibandwe bule, angachesa matso kuirinda. 40 12:40 Math' 24:42Bai ninwi nanwi dziikeni thayari wakathi wosi, kwani Mwana wa Mudamu andakudza wakathi musiwoudhania.” 41 Kusikira vizho P'et'ero akimuza akimwamba, “Bwana, fuambe rino fumbo unarambira siswi kare, hedu unarambira at'u osini!” 42 12:42-44 19:17-19Ye Bwana akidzigidzya akiamba, “Muhumiki mwaminifu na mulachu ni yuyahu ariye akirichirwa ahumiki andziye ni bwanawe aatsundze na kuap'a chakurya here vimalikanazho, andahenda vizho. 43 Bai baha iye muhumiki iye ariye bwanawe, andakudzamuthekeza anahenda vizho. 44 Namwambira ujeri, andamuhala na amuhendye akale muimirizi wa zho vit'u zhosi arizho nazho. 45 Ela ye muhumiki akiamba kimoyo-moyo, ‘Naona ye bwana wangu k'andauya vikara,’ bai akwache kuapiga o ahumiki andziye ache kwa alume, na athuwe kurya na kunwa na kurea. 46 Bai ye bwana wa muhumiki yuyu andauya siku asiyodhania na saa asiyoimanya. Naye afikaho andakudzamuhala na amutose visiku-visiku na amwike hamwenga na at'u masiokuluhirika. 47 12:47 Jako 4:17Muhumiki ambaye anamanya amalazho ye bwanawe, ela ko k'adzithayarisha hedu kuhenda zho ahendzazho ye mubomuwe, be muhumiki iye andapigwa ut'u wa kwangalaza. 48 Ela muhumiki ariye k'amanya kala ye bwanawe anahendzani, kidza ahende maut'u garigo ganav̱irya apigwe, bai muhumiki iye k'andapigwa zhomu. Kila mut'u ariyegerwa vinji, andaiswa vinji, kidza iye ariyeikirwa amana vit'u vinji, bai mut'u iye andamalwa vinji zaidhi.”
Jesu anaamba k'adzire arehe dheri
Math'ayo 10:34-36
49 Jesu akiamba, “Mut'u mimi nakudzaigwiza moho ii dhunia, nami naaza ukale hatha ni gwuu kare! 50 12:50 Mar 10:38Ela bai nina ubat'izo uriwo niniukirire, yani kifwa, nami be nabudzyika muhaka kit'u kiki kindihomarigizika! 51 12:51 2:14Nwi enye munaona nakudza nirehe dheri hano dhuniani? Hatha! Sidzire nirehe dheri, ela namwambira nakudza nihende at'u magazhane. 52 12:52-53 14:26; 21:16; Math' 24:10; Mar 13:12Kwani kula vivi vikara na kuenderera at'u atsano a nyumba mwenga mandagazhana. Ahahu mandakira kumwenga na o airi mandakira upande wao. 53 12:53 Mik 7:6Bimut'u andathengana na mwanawe wa kilume, ye mwana wa kilume naye andathengana na babaye. Mimut'u andathengana na mwanawe wa kiche na ye mwanangwa wa kiche andathengana na mameye. Mizhala andathengana na mukaza mwanawe, na ye mukaza mwana wa mut'u andathengana na mizhalaye.”
Kumanya makathi
Math'ayo 16:2-3; 5:25-26
54 Jesu akinena na ro kundi ra at'u akiamba, “Ninwi mukiona ingu ridzagit'ana ko mutserero wa dzuwa nikuamba, ‘Indanya’ na ikanya jeri. 55 H'aya, na p'eho ya mwakani ipigaho, ninwi nikuamba, ‘Kundahenda kazikazi,’ na vikakala vizho jeri. 56 At'u a kudzihendya nwi! Munamanya kulola mulunguni na ts'i na mukamanya kundahendeka ut'u wani. Ela mbona miv̱ano ya makathi hurigo k'amumanya kuilola?
57 “Kwanoni k'amulola nwi enye mukamanya ririro karakara kuhenda? 58 Mut'u akikala anakuhirika kotini, jeza here wadimazho upatane naye mo ngirani. Kala k'undahenda vizho, iye andakuv̱urura muhaka kwa jaji, jaji akulazhe kwa shikari, na shikari akutsuhe jela. 59 Nami nakwambira k'undambola uwe muhaka ugonye kuriha p'esa zosi.”
