6
1 6:1 9:14H'aya bai, nafugakire gago mafundisho ga kwandza-kwandza furigofundishwa dzulu za Masihi, na futhuwe mafundisho makomu garigo gandafukumaza. Fusigauyire bule garyahu mafundisho ga musinji. Na gaga ndo mafundisho ga musinji: Kudziutha na kuricha mahendo mai-mai, kumukuluhira Mulungu, 2 6:2 Joh 3:25-26; Mahe 19:1-6malagizo dzulu za kubat'izwa na kuikirwa mikono, kufufuka kwa at'u mariofwa, na hukumu ya kare na kare. 3 Na Mulungu akihendza, fundagathalala fukumale.
4 6:4-6 2:1-3; 5:8; 10:26-29; 12:25; Math' 12:31; 1 Joh 5:16Kwani k'azhadimikika bule kwa at'u mariomukana Jesu na kumuricha Mulungu kamare, kuudzywa lusha madze mathubu kaheri. Kwa kukala ao ni at'u marioumanya ujeri, makithatha kigerwa cha Mulungu na makihokera Roho Mweri dza siswi. 5 H'aya zho mbai bai, ao mahokera neno ra Mulungu na makirithatha udzo wa kwakwe, kidza makimanya nguvu za urumwengu udzawo. 6 Ela hatha bada ya kuhokera maut'u gago gosi, o mamukane Jesu na kumuricha Mulungu kamare, mandakala manamusulubisha kaheri ye Mwana wa Mulungu, na kumuaibisha mbere za at'u, kasidi. Kwa vizho k'amadima kuudzywa lusha madze mathubu kaheri.
7 Munda uriwo unapigwa ni v̱ula kwenda na unazhala mavuno gamalwago ni o ak'urima mariourima, Mulungu ni kuuhasa. 8 6:8 Math' 13:7, 22Ela kala undamera kimbiri na kidunga-dunga, munda uwo k'auna mana yoyosi, na k'aukure na kulaniwa ni Mulungu na mwishowe uhende kuochwa kamare.
9 Asena, fukinena vivi, si kwamba vizho ndo vindizhokala kwenu, hatha. Siswi funa hakika kukala kuna maut'u madzo zaidhi gambolago kahi za uwo wokolwi wenu. 10 6:10 10:32-34; 13:1; 1 Joh 4:7-12, 19-21Kwani Mulungu ni muhachi na k'andasahau bule zho murizho hangu chaho hatha vivi, munaahumikira na kuanyesa mahendzo at'ue kwa ut'u wa kwakwe iye. 11 Fukahendza zhomu kukala, kila mumwenga wenu, akale na moyo uwo wa chadi, na uhakika wa kukala garyahu maut'u mugatharizago gandakamilika, muhaka mwisho. 12 6:12 11:1-12; Luk 21:19Musikale akaha bule, ela aigizeni ao mario kwa kukuluhira na kuvumirira kwao mandapata zho Mulungu arizholaga kukala andaap'a.
Mulungu andathimiza vilaganezhe
13 6:13-14 Mwan 22:16-17; Aebu 11:12Mulungu arihoika kilagane na Burahemu walaha kwa dzinare mwenye, mana were k'ahana mut'u mubomu kumukira iye, ariye were anadima kulaha kwa dzinare. 14 Bai waamba kukala, “Jeri wenye mimi nindakuhasa na niluhende lukolo lwa kwako lukale lubomu.” 15 Na Burahemu wavumirira hatha zho arizhokala Mulungu wamulaga kukala andamup'a, akivihokera jeri. 16 6:16 Kumbo 22:11; Math' 5:34; 23:16-22Mut'u akilaha, nikulaha kwa dzina ra mut'u ariye dzuluze, na kulaha kuko nikugonya k'anani zosi. 17 Na Mulungu were anamala kuahakikishira ao mandiohokera zho vilagane kukala k'andagaluza nia bule. Kwa vizho akilaha kukala andavihenda. 18 6:18 Hesab 23:19Kwa vizho Mulungu wahenda vit'u viri, waika kilagane na akilaha kukala andakithimiza. Na vizho vit'u viri k'avidima kugaluka, kwa kukala iye k'anena ulongo bule. Kwa ut'u uwo bai, siswi furiomuhendya akale ch'imbiriro yehu, funagerwa moyo zhomu kukala yo th'amaa furiyoikirwa mbere zehu be fuigwire ngwa kamare. 19 6:19 3:6; Arum 5:5; Aebu 9:3Uhakika uu furiwo nawo unahendya roho zehu zikale imara, salama, na zenye nguvu here dau ridzirogwaga nanga. Kidza uhakika uu undafulongoza hatha futhalale yo p'azia na fwangire kahi za ho hat'u haeri*6:19 Kahi za Hekalu ra Ayahudi kwere kuna p'azia iriyogazha hat'u haeri, na Hat'u Haeri Kukira Kueri Kosi. Hat'u Haeri Kukira Kueri Kosi kwere kwangira mulombi mubomu h'akeye. ha Mulungu. 20 6:20 4:14; Melikizedeki: Mwan 14:17-20; Zabu 110:4; Aebu 5:6, 10; 7:1-4Jesu wafuthangulia kare kahi za ho hat'u haeri ha Mulungu kwa ut'u wehu siswi. Naye wahenda vizho bada ya kukala adzahendywa mulombi wehu mubomu wa kare na kare, mwenye ulombi karakara na wa Melikizedeki.†6:20 Ukihendza kumanya dzulu za Melikizedeki shoma ‘Maelezo ga maneno maalumu’ kuko mwisho.
