28
Kufufuka kwa Jesu
Mariko 16:1-8; Luka 24:1-12; Johana 20:1-10
1 Sabato irihokala idzakira, siku ya kwandza ya wiki na madzach'a maitsi genye, Maryamu Magidalena, na ye Maryamu mungine, marauka kwenda ko mbirani makailole. 2 Na lola haho henye ts'i ikithethema zhomu zhenye kwa kukala malaika wa Bwana watharamuka kula dzulu akidzaripingilisa ro iwe ra kusindikira yo mbira, akirikelesira. 3 Uso wa malaika iye were unang'ala here lumethe, na nguwo za kwakwe were ni nyaruhe tse-tse-tse. 4 Aryahu shikari mariokala manarinda yo mbira makivinwa ni miri kwa woga hatha makikala here at'u madziofwa. 5 Ela ye malaika akinena na o anaache akiamba, “Musigohe bule. Ninamanya kukala munamumala ye Jesu ariyesulubiwa, 6 28:6 16:21bai k'aho bule haha. Adzafufuka here arizhonena. Hatha ndzoni muhalole ho ariholazwa. 7 28:7 26:32Na mukila haha, yerani malo mukaambire anafundzie kukala, ‘Adzafufuka kula kifwani na lola anamuthangulia kwenda Galilaya nanwi mundakwenda mukamone kuko.’ H'aya nidzamwambira mino.”
8 Bai o anaache mahauka haraka haryahu mbirani, kuno mana raha jeri-jeri, idzaho wo woga were k'audzaambola. Mauka malo mende makaambire o anafundzi dzulu za maut'u gago. 9 Marihokala makwendani, lola Jesu akiambolera gafula mbere zao, akialamusa akiamba, “Voro.” Na lola o anaache makimusongerera makimugwira go magulu makimuvoya. 10 28:10 12:50; Joh 20:17Jesu akinena akiamba, “Musigohe bule. Endani mukaambire o ndugu zangu mende Galilaya, nao mandanona kuko.”
Shikari manendapiga ripoti kwa alombi
11 Vikara marihokala o anaache makwendani, lola shikari angine kahi za aryahu mariokala manarinda yo mbira, mangira mumo Jerusalemu machendaambira o alombi abomu kila kit'u kirichokala kidzahendeka. 12 Bai aryahu alombi makithunganana na o athumia a Kiyahudi na makipanga kukala maahonge o shikari. Kwa vizho makiap'a p'esa nyinji zenye, 13 28:13 27:64gonya makiambira kukala, “Ambani kukala usiku hudzihokala hurere, anafundzie madziremuiya makenda naye. 14 Na luwali akivisikira hundanena naye siswi hatha athosheke p'ore mukangira matatani.” 15 Bai o shikari makizihala zo p'esa na makihenda here marizholagizwa. Na lola ulongo uu watsamuka kahi za Ayahudi hatha vivi.
Jesu anahuma anafundzie makahubiri kila hat'u
Mariko 16:14-18; Luka 24:36-49; Johana 20:19-23; Mahendo 1:6-8
16 28:16 5:1; 26:32; 28:10Badaye aryahu anafundzi kumi na mumwenga a Jesu mauka kwenda Galilaya. Makwenda vivii hatha makifika wo murima ariolagiza Jesu kukala mathunganane. 17 28:17 14:31Bai marihomona, madzigwaga mbereze makimuvoya, idzaho angine were machere mana mioyo miri-miri. 18 28:18 Dani 7:14; Math' 11:27Jesu akiasongerera hehi na akinena nao akiamba, “Mimi nagerwa wadimi wosi dzulu mulunguni na ts'i. 19 28:19 At'u a makabila gosi: 10:5-6; 24:14; Mar 16:15; Luk 24:47; Ubat'izo: Math' 3:15; Mar 16:16; Mahe 2:38; 8:36; 1 Akor 1:13-16; Aefe 4:5; Joh 3:22; 4:1-2; Arum 6:3-4; Baba, Mwana na Roho: 2 Akor 13:13Kwa vizho endani mukaahendye at'u a makabila gosi makale anafundzi angu. Muabat'ize kahi za dzina ra Baba, na ra Mwana, na ra Roho Mweri. 20 28:20 1:23; 5:2-7, 27; 11:28-30; 18:20Na muafundishe magagwire maut'u gosi nirigomulagiza. Na lola mimi nindakala hamwenga nanwi siku zosini muhaka mwisho wa dhunia.”
