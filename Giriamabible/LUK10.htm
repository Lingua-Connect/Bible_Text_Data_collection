10
Jesu anahuma anafundzie mirongo mifungahe na airi
1 10:1-12 9:1-6Bada ya gaga, Bwana watsagula anafundzi angine mirongo mifungahe na airi. Gonya akiahuma airi-airi mende kimbere kahi za kila mudzi na kila hat'u ambaho iye mwenye were k'avindatoa andakwenda. 2 Bai akiambira, “Mavuno ni manji, ela ahendzi a kazi ni achache. Kwa vizho bai muvoyeni ye Bwana wa mavuno, ahume ahendzi a kazi madze mavune. 3 H'aya, endani! Lolani ninamuhuma here ana a ng'ondzi kahikahi za mbawa. 4 10:4 22:35; 2 Athaw 4:29Musitsukule p'ochi, mukoba, hedu virahu. Kidza mo ngirani musingamize wakathi kulamusa-lamusa at'u.
5 10:5-6 2:14“Kahi za nyumba yoyosi mundiyongira kwandza ambani, ‘Dheri narikale kahi za nyumba ii.’ 6 Bai mukikala muna mut'u wa dheri mumo, be dheri renu rindakala dzuluze na kala k'amuna mut'u wa dheri, bai rindamuuyira enye. 7 10:7 1 T'imo 5:18; 1 Akor 9:6-14Nanwi toani kahi za nyumba iyo murye na munwe chochosi mandichomwambozera, mana muhendzi wa kazi anav̱irya kupata marihoge. 8 Mudzi wowosi mundiongira na mukikaribishwa, ryani chochosi mundichombozerwa. 9 10:9-11 11:2Hozani o akongo mario haho na muambire vivi, ‘Uthawali wa Mulungu udzamufikira hehi.’ 10 Ela mwangiraho kahi za mudzi na musikaribishwe endani kahi za zo barabaraze na muambe, 11 10:11 Mahe 13:51; 18:6‘Hatha ro vumbi ra uwo mudzi wenu ridzirohugwira, hunarikut'a kunyesa kukala mundamanya enye; kidza manyani vivi, kukala wo uthawali wa Mulungu udzafika hehi.’ 12 10:12 Mwan 18:20-21; Math' 10:15Bai namwambira kamare kukala siku ya mwisho vindakala vii-vii zhomu kwa mudzi uwo, kukira zho vindizhokala kwa Sodomu.”
Midzi iriyo k'aikuluhirire
Math'ayo 11:20-24
13 10:13-14 Isa 23:1-16Jesu akienderera kunena akiamba, “Shauri yenu ninwi at'u a Korazini na Beth'isaida. Kwani kala yo miujiza niriyoihenda ko kwenu be naihenda kahi za midzi ya Tiro na Sidoni be*10:13 Tiro na Sidoni, ni midzi ambayo were inatoa at'u mario si Ayahudi, na midzi ya Korazini na Beth'isaida were inatoa Ayahudi. andziyenu ngere mavala nguwo za magunia, na kukala ivuni kapindi kunyesa kuthubu kwao. 14 Ela yo siku ya hukumu be vindakala baha o at'u a midzi ya Tiro na Sidoni kaheri kuriko ninwi. 15 10:15 Isa 14:13, 15H'aya, nwi at'u a Kaperinaumu nanwi, munaona mundanulwa hatha mulunguni? Hatha bule, ninwi mundatserezwa hatha kuzimu.”
16 10:16 9:48; Math' 18:5; Mar 9:37; Joh 13:20Jesu akiambira o anafundzie akiamba, “Mut'u amusikiraye ninwi, ananisikira mimi, na mut'u amukahalaye ninwi, ananikahala mimi. Anikahalaye mimi, anamukahala iye ariyenihuma.” H'aya makiuka machenda.
Kuuya kwa aryahu anafundzi mirongo mifungahe na airi
17 10:17 9:10; Mar 6:30Badaye aryahu anafundzi mirongo mifungahe na airi mauya kuno mana raha makiamba, “Bwana, lola bewe hatha o p'ep'o manahusikira siswi kwa dzinaro.” 18 10:18 Joh 12:31; Ugun 12:9Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Namona Shethani anagwa kula mulunguni dza here lumethe. 19 10:19 Zabu 91:13Lolani, namup'a wadimi wa kuzhoga nyoka na mah'ambini na wadimi wa kuzishinda nguvu zosi za iye hadhui. K'akuna chochosi kindichomulumiza. 20 10:20 Kumbo 32:32; Afil 4:3; Ugun 3:5Ela hamwenga na vizho musihende raha kwa kukala o p'ep'o manamusirikira, ela hendani raha kwa kukala madzina genu gaorwa ko mulunguni.”
Jesu anamulazhira muv̱era Mulungu
Math'ayo 11:25-27; 13:16-17
21 10:21 4:18; Math' 11:25Wakathi uwo Jesu wadzalwa ni fwahiro kukirira na Roho Mweri akiamba, “Nakulazhira muv̱era h'ewe Baba, Bwana wa dzulu na ts'i, kwa kukala maut'u gaga wagafitsa alachu na enye akili, ukigagunulira madanga ga ana. Ni karakara Baba, mana vizho ndizho urizhohendza.”
22 10:22 Math' 11:27Jesu akienderera kunena akiamba, “Maut'u gosini gaikwa mikononi mwangu ni Baba. K'akuna mut'u amanyaye ye Mwana ni h'ani asihokala ye Baba, na k'akuna mut'u amanyaye ye Baba ni h'ani asihokala ye Mwana na aryahu ambao ye Mwana anahendza kuang'azira ye Baba.”
23 Gonya akiagalukira o anafundzie akiambira kinjama akiamba, “Baha ao maonao maut'u gaga mugaonago. 24 10:24 1 P'et 1:10-12Mana namwambira kamare manabii anji na athawali mathamani kuona gaga mugaonago, ela k'amagaonere, kidza mathamani masikire gago mugasikirago ela k'amagasikirire bule.”
Fumbo ra Musamaria mudzo
25 10:25 18:18; Math' 19:16; Mar 10:17Lola siku mwenga, mwalimu mumwenga wa Sheria warungarara kumujeza Jesu na swali akiamba, “Mwalimu, nihendedze hatha niupate uzima wa kare na kare?” 26 Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Kwani kahi za yo Sheria, mwaorwani? Na unavishomadze we?” 27 10:27 Kumbu 6:5; Alaw 19:18; Math' 22:37, 39Akimudzigidzya akiamba, “ ‘Muhendze Bwana Mulunguo na moyowo wosi, na rohoro rosi, na nguvuzo zosi, na akilizo zosi,’ na ‘Muhendze mwandziyo here udzihendzazho mwenye.’ ” 28 10:28 Alaw 18:5; Arum 10:5; Agal 3:12; Joh 13:17Jesu akimwamba, “Udzadzigidzya karakara, bai henda vizho nawe undaishi.” 29 Ela yuyahu mwalimu wa Sheria were anahendza aonekane kukala ro swalire were ni ra mana, kwa vizho akimuza Jesu akiamba, “Na yuno jirani wangu ni hiye?”
30 Bai kahi za kudzigidzya, Jesu waamba, “Kwere kuna mwanamulume mumwenga ariyembola Jerusalemu na akikala anatharamuka kwenda Jeriko. Bai mumo ngirani akigwavukirwa ni akora nao mamupiga na makimuhoka zo nguwoze na makimuricha haho ngirani k'adzimanya, gonya thio, machenda. 31 Kwa bahathi mbidzo mulombi mumwenga akitharamuka na ngira iyo vizho, ela arihomona be hatha wahenda kukira na k'anda kamare. 32 Bai Mulawi naye arihofika haho na kumona ye mut'u, wahenda vizho zhenye naye, wakira na k'anda. 33 10:33 Samaria: Math' 10:5; Luk 9:52; 17:11, 16; Joh 4:9, 39-40; Mahe 1:8; 8:1, 5, 14-17, 25; 9:31Ela Musamaria mumwenga ariye hatha si Muyahudi kamare were a kahi za safarize arihofika haho arihokala ye mut'u, wadzalwa ni mbazi arihomona. 34 Bai akimusongerera na achendamumwagira mafuha na divai mo marangani na akimufunga bandeji. Vikara yuyahu Musamaria were ana ndzowewe, bai wamuhala ye mupigwa na akimukweza kahi za ndzowe wa kwakwe na achenda naye vivii muhaka kahi za nyumba ya kukala asafiri ambako wakwendamutsundzat'o. 35 Siku ya hiriye ye Musamaria wamboza p'esa akimup'a ye mwenye yo nyumba ya kukala asafiri na akimwamba, ‘Mutsundze yuyu, na kukikala na garama zozosi nyingine, ndakudza nikuujire haho nikikala nidzauya.’ ”
36 Gonya Jesu akimuza ye mwalimu wa Sheria akiamba, “Vikara kahi za ano ahahu, unaona ni h'ani we ariye wakala ni jirani wa ye ariyegwavukirwa ni akora?” 37 Ye mwalimu wa Sheria akidzigidzya akiamba, “Ni yuyahu ariyemuonera mbazi ye mut'u.” Jesu akimwamba, “H'aya uwe nawe enda ukahende dza vizho.”
Jesu anaatsemberera Marith'a na Maryamu
38 10:38-40 Joh 11:2; 12:2-3Jesu na anafundzie marihokala ma kahi za safari zao, mafika kahi za kadzidzi kamwenga, bai mwanamuche mumwenga aifwaye Marith'a akimukaribisha ko kwakwe mudzini. 39 Marith'a yuyu were ana nduguye aifwaye Maryamu. Maryamu wathuwa kukeresi ho ts'i hehi na magulu ga Bwana kumusirikiza gago arigokala ananena. 40 Ela Marith'a akikala anabudzyika na zo kazi za kuathayarishira o ajeni. Kwa vizho achenda kwa Jesu, achendamwamba, “Bwana, k'ujali we zho zha kukala yuno ndugu yangu adzanirichira kazi zosini h'akiyangu? Mwambe adze anavize!” 41 Ye Bwana akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Marith'a asena, una wasiwasi na kubudzyika na maut'u manji, 42 ela ut'u uriwo unahendzekana ni ut'u umwenga thu, Maryamu adzatsagula kit'u ambacho ni kidzo zaidhi nacho k'andakihokwa kamare.”
