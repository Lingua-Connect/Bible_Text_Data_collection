4
Mavoyo kuko mulunguni
1 Bada ya vizho, natsuha matso, na lola nikiona muryango uriwokala u lwazu ko mulunguni. Na wo mumiro here wa gunda niriwokala nidzausikira ho kwandza ukinamba, “Ndzo haha dzulu, na nindakwanyesa zho ambazho ni lazima vithimire bada ya vivi.” 2 4:2-3 5:7; Zabu 11:4; 103:19Na mara mwenga kare nikikala kahi za Roho, na lola ko mulunguni, kwere kuna kihi cha endzi na mut'u ariyekala adzakikeresiza. 3 4:3 Izik 1:26-28Ye mut'u were anang'ala-ng'ala here mawe ga th'amani, na cho kihi cha endzi chere kidzadengerekwa ni kisiki cha v̱ula kirichokala kinang'ala here iwe ra th'amani ra chani kitsi. 4 4:4 Azhere: 5:5, 8, 10; 7:11, 13; 11:16; 14:3; 19:4; Isa 24:23; Nyaruhe: 6:11Pia cho kihi cha endzi were kidzadengerekwa ni vihi vingine zha endzi mirongo miri na vine. Vihi vivi zhere vidzakelesirwa ni azhere mirongo miri na ane, mariokala madzavala nguwo nyaruhe na taji za dhahabu. 5 4:5 8:5; 11:19; 16:18; 17:9; Kumbo 9:24; 19:16; Izik 1:13-14Kwambola lumethe, na sauthi, na kigwaragwara kula kahi za cho kihi cha endzi. Mbere za cho kihi cha endzi kwere kuna mataa mafungahe garigokala ganaaka. Mataa gaga ni zo roho fungahe za Mulungu. 6 4:6 15:2; Izik 1:22; Zhumbe vine: 5:6, 8, 11; 6:1; 7:11; 14:3; 15:7; 19:4; Izik 1:5-12; 10:14Kidza kwere kuna ut'u here bahari ya kilolo iriyokala inanyesa lwazu-lwazu. Haho kahikahi kukidengereka cho kihi cha endzi kwere kuna zhumbe vine zha moyo virizhokala vi thele matso mbere na nyuma. 7 Chumbe cha kwandza were ni here simba. Cha hiri were ni here kidzao cha ng'ombe. Cha hahu were kina sura here ya binadamu, na cha ne were ni here mwee abururukaye. 8 4:8 1:17; 11:17; Isa 6:2-3; Izik 1:18; 10:12Kahi za zho zhumbe vine, kila chumbe, were kina mahaha mahandahu, na matso mwiri muzima. Zhumbe vivi zhaimba usiku na mutsana bila kunyamala. Zhaimba vikiamba,
“Mweri, mweri, mweri
Bwana Mulungu mwenye nguvu zosi.
Ariyekalako, ariyeko, na andiyekudza.”
9 Na wakathi wosi virihokala zho zhumbe vine vinamwimbira maira ga nguma na ishima na muv̱era ye ariyekala akelesi dzulu za cho kihi cha endzi, aishiye kare na kare, 10 o azhere mirongo miri na ane magwa mbere za ye ariyekala akelesi dzulu za cho kihi cha endzi makimuvoya ye aishiye tha kare na kare. Matsuha ts'i taji zao mbere za cho kihi cha endzi na makiimba makiamba,
11 4:11 Mwan 1:1, 31; Ugun 5:9, 12; 11:15, 17; 12:10; 15:3-4; 19:6“Mulungu wehu uriye Bwana wehu
unav̱irya kuhokera nguma na ishima na wadimi.
Kwa kukala waumba vit'u zhosi,
na kwa kuhendzako mwenye zhaumbwa vikaleko.”
