18
Jesu ananena dzulu za ubomu
Mariko 9:33-37; Luka 9:46-48
1 18:1 Luk 22:24Vikara wakathi uryahu, anafundzi makimuthuwa Jesu makimwamba, “Kahi za uthawali wa mulunguni mubomu ni hiye?” 2 Bai kabila Jesu k'adzadzigidzya, akiiha muhoho muthithe akimuimisha ho mbere zao. 3 18:3 19:14; Mar 10:15; Luk 18:17Akiamba, “Namwambira ujeri wenye, kala k'amundagaluka na kukala here ahoho athithe, k'amundawangira ng'o wo uthawali wa mulunguni. 4 18:4 23:12Kila andegaluka na kudzitsereza dza zho zha yuyu muhoho, iye nde mut'u mubomu kukira osi kahi za uthawali wa mulunguni. 5 18:5 Luk 10:16Na mut'u yoyosi amuhokeraye muhoho dza yuyu kwa dzina rangu ananihokera mimi.”
Maut'u gadimago kumudzya mut'u nyuma
Mariko 9:42-48; Luka 17:1-2
6 “Ela mut'u yoyosi andiyemuhendya mumwenga wa aa athithe manikuluhirao ahende dambi, ni baha afungwe lwalwa lwa singo atsufwe ko kahikahi ya bahari kwenye.
7 “Shauri yao ao mandioahendya andziyao mahende dambi. Majezo ni hatha gakale mumu dhuniani, ela shauriye iye andiyegareha. 8 18:8-9 5:29-30Kwa ut'u uwo wenye bai, mukonowo ukikuhendya uhende dambi, utose uutsuhe. Hedu kigulucho kikikuhendya uhende dambi, kitose ukitsuhe. Kwani baha wangire uzimani na mukono mumwenga hedu na kigulu kimwenga, kuriko utsufwe kahi za moho wa kare na kare na mikono miri hedu magulu mairi. 9 Na dzitsoro rikikuhendya uhende dambi, rishukule uritsuhe. Kwani baha wangire uzimani na ch'ongo kuriko utsufwe kahi za moho wa Jehanamu.
10 18:10-14 Joh 10:11-15“Dzimanyirireni sana p'ore mukaadharau ahoho athithe, kwani malaika a kwao ko mulunguni ma mbere za Baba ariye mulunguni.” [ 11 Kwani Mwana wa Mudamu wakudzaokola kirichongamika.]
Fumbo ra ng'ondzi iriyongamika
Luka 15:3-7
12 Jesu akienderera kunena akiamba, “Mut'u akikala ana ng'ondzi gana mwenga gonya mwenga ikangamike, munaona nikuhendadze? Samba nikuziricha zo mirongo chenda na chenda akendaimala yo idziyongamika hatha akayona? 13 Na ninamwambira ujeri, akiyona andakala na raha nayo zhomu hatha kukira zo mirongo chenda na chenda zirizo k'azingamikire. 14 18:14 6:10Na vizho ndo virizho hatha kwa Mulungu Babiyehu wa mulunguni. Iye, k'endzi aa ahoho athithe mangamike hatha mumwenga.”
Jesu anaambira anafundzi makale makikanyana
Luka 17:3
15 18:15 Alaw 19:17; Agal 6:1“Ndugu akikukosera, muthuwe wiri wenu, ukamuzhagazhe umwambire makosage. Akigakubali ni vidzo, undakala udzamupata. 16 18:16 Kumbu 19:15; Joh 8:17; 2 Akor 13:1; 1 T'imo 5:19Kala k'andagakubali, mala mut'u mumwenga hedu airi mwende nyosini ili kila neno rindironenwa rikale na mashahidhi airi hedu ahahu. 17 18:17 Tit 3:10Akikala k'andakubali, enda ukariambire kanisa. Akikubali, vidzo, k'akubalire, muhalire mut'u yuyahu dza ungazhomuhalira mut'u asiyemumanya Mulungu hedu here muthozi wa kodi. 18 18:18 16:19; Joh 20:23Nami namwambira ujeri kukala kila mundichokifunga haha dhuniani hatha ko mulunguni nako kindakala kidzafungwa, na kila mundichokivugula haha dhuniani hatha ko mulunguni nako kindakala kidzavugulwa. 19 18:19 7:7Kidza at'u airi kahi zenu makikubaliana dzulu za ut'u wowosi mandiwouvoya haha dhuniani, Babiyenu wa mulunguni andaahendera. 20 18:20 28:20Kwani at'u airi hedu ahahu mathungananaho kwa dzina rangu nindakala hamwenga nao.”
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kusameheana
21 Jesu arihogonya kunena, P'et'ero akimuza akimwamba, “Bwana, mwandzangu akinikosera kwenda-kwenda, nimusamehe hatha k'ana nyingahi? Vidze nimusamehe k'ana fungahe?” 22 18:22 Mwan 4:24Jesu akimudzigidzya akimwamba, “Hatha bule, simusamehe k'ana fungahe thu, musamehe k'ana mirongo mifungahe mara fungahe.”
Fumbo ra mut'u asiyesamehe andziye
23 18:23 25:19Kidza Jesu akiamba, “Ndo hatha uthawali wa mulunguni udzahalana na muthawali mumwenga ariyekala anamala kuhenda isabu za p'esa arizoap'a ahumikie. 24 K'ona shuhuli iyo irihoandza thu, akireherwa muhumikiwe mumwenga ariyekala anamwisa t'alant'a alifu kumi. 25 Na kwa kukala were k'adima kuziriha, ye muthawali akiamuru kukala aguzwe hamwenga na adamue osi, na vit'uzhe zhosi, ili zo p'esa zindizopatikana zirihe ro dheni. 26 Ela ye muhumiki akimugwerera maguluni ye muthawali, akimuvoya akiamba, ‘Bwana, kala radhi nami unip'e muda nami ndakuriha p'esazo zosi.’ 27 18:27 Luk 7:42K'uzhona ye muthawali moyo ukimutika madzi hatha ro dheni akimusamehe, na akimuvugula akimurichira. 28 Ela kula haho kare, achendamona muhumiki mwandziwe ariyekala anamwisa dinari gana mwenga thu. K'uzhona akimugwavukira kare, gwa, akimugwira mumiro. Cha ‘Niriha p'esa zangu.’ 29 Bai ye mwandziwe akimugwerera maguluni akimwamba, ‘Kala radhi nami unip'e muda nami ndakuriha p'esazo,’ 30 ela akikahala kamare. Akimuhala akimuhirika jela achendafungwa muhaka ho andihomarigiza kuriha. 31 Vikara ahumiki andziye marihoona viryahu mashikithika zhomu hatha machendamusema kwa muthawali. 32 Bai, muthawali arihosikira uv̱oro uryahu, wamwiha mara mwenga yuyahu muhumiki ariyekala wamusamehe akimwamba, ‘We kumba u muthiriri vivi we! Waniririra nami nikikusamehe dheniro rosi, 33 18:33 5:7ela mbona we ye mwandziyo k'umuonerere mbazi dza vizho nirizhokuonera mbazi?’ 34 18:34 5:26K'uzhona ye muthawali akitsukirwa zhomu hatha akiaiha shikari a jela madze mamuhale makamufunge muhaka ho andihomarigiza kuriha ro dhenire. 35 18:35 6:12, 15; Aefe 4:32; Akol 3:13; Jako 2:13Na vizho zhenye ndizho andizhohenda Baba wa mulunguni, kala k'amundaasamehe andziyenu na moyo mumwenga.”
