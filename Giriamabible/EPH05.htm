5
1 5:1 Math' 5:48; 1 Joh 3:1Bai mwigeni Mulungu, here anae amuhendzao. 2 5:2 3:17-18; 4:2, 15-16; 1 Akor 8:1; 2 Akor 5:14; Agal 6:10; Afil 2:2; Akol 2:2; 3:14-15; Sadaka: Agal 1:4; Aebu 10:5-10Ishini kwa kuhendzana, dza viryahu Masihi arizhohuhendza siswi, hatha akidzilazha akifwa, here sadaka ya muruk'e mudzo kwa Mulungu kwa ut'u wehu siswi.
3 5:3-5 Agal 5:19-215:3 Akol 3:5Bai kwa kukala mudzauya mu at'u a Mulungu, k'amuv̱irya kuhadzwa na maut'u ga uzindzi bule, hedu maitsi-maitsi, na k'amuv̱irya kumbozera dzitso cha mut'u. 4 5:4 4:29; Akol 3:8K'amuhendzwa kunena maneno ga aibu, hedu ga kizuzu, k'amuv̱irya kuhenda madhe mai, mana gago k'agafwaha, ela hat'u ha vizho mushukuruni Mulungu. 5 5:5 Arum 5:17Bai ninwi manyani kamare kukala, muzindzi, hedu mut'u ahendaye maitsi-maitsi, hedu mut'u mwenye dzitso (mut'u dza iye ni muvoyi wa vizuka), k'andangira kamare kahi za uthawali wa Masihi na Mulungu. 6 5:6 Arum 1:18; Aefe 2:3; Akol 2:4, 8; 3:6Mut'u asimukenge kwa kumuahidhi maut'u ga ulongo, mana k'oro za Mulungu zindaadzirira ao at'u mario manahenda maut'u gaga, kwa kukala k'amamwagoha Mulungu. 7 Kwa vizho musishirikiane na at'u dza vizho aa.
8 5:8 Haho kapindi: 2:3, 11-12; 4:17-18, 22; Akol 3:7; Tit 3:3; Kahi za mulangaza: Joh 8:12; Akol 1:13; 1 P'et 2:9Haho kapindi ninwi mwere mu kizani, ela vivi bai mu kahi za mulangaza wa Bwana, kwa vizho ishini here at'u mario mulangazani mav̱iryazho kuishi. 9 5:9 Agal 5:22-23Kwani at'u mario mulangazani ni kuhenda maut'u madzo-madzo, ga hachi na ga ujeri. 10 5:10 Arum 12:2; 1 Ath'es 5:22Dzifundisheni mumanye ni ut'u wani umufwahizao Bwana. 11 Na kidza musihusike na maut'u ga kizani, garigo k'agana faidha yoyosi, ela hat'u ha vizho gang'azeni. 12 Mana gago mahendago kisiri-siri, hatha k'aganeneka. 13 5:13 Joh 3:20-21Ela maut'u gaking'azwa, nikupigwa ni mulangaza na gakaonewa lwazu. 14 5:14 Isa 26:19; 60:1; Arum 13:11; Jako 5:8Kwani mulangaza ndiwo uhendao kit'u kikaoneka lwazu. Ndosa at'u ni kuamba,
“Lamuka we urereye,
fufuka kula kuzimu,
na Masihi andakung'alira.”
15 Kwa vizho bai, dzimanyirireni sana zho muishizho. Musiishi here at'u azuzu, ishini here at'u alachu. 16 5:16 Akol 4:5Humirani wakathi wenu t'ot'ot'o, mana siku zizi ni mbii. 17 5:17 1:17; Arum 12:2Kwa vizho bai musikale azuzu, ela manyani mahendzo ga Bwana ni noni. 18 5:18 Ndhum 23:31-35; Arum 13:13Musiree bule, mana divai indamwangamiza, ela hat'uhe, dzazwani Roho Mweri, 19 5:19-20 Zabu 33:2-3; Akol 3:16-17; Aebu 13:15na muimbirane zaburi, p'ambiyo na maira ga kiroho, na mioyoni mwenu mukale munamuimbira Bwana na kumushangilia. 20 Lazhani muv̱era kwa Mulungu Baba kila wakathi, na kwa kila kit'u, kwa dzina ra Bwana wehu Jesu Masihi. 21 5:21–6:9 Akol 3:18–4:1; 1 P'et 2:18–3:7; 1 T'imo 2:1-13; Tit 2:1-10; 1 Joh 2:12-175:21 1 P'et 5:5Na kidza kwa ut'u wa kumwogoha Masihi, kila mumwenga wenu ni adzitsereze kwa mwandziwe.
Mitoere ya midzini
Akolosai 3:18–4:1
22 5:22-24 Akol 3:18; Tit 2:5; 1 P'et 3:15:22 1 Akor 14:34-35Ninwi ache, asikireni alume enu, dza zho mumusikirazho Bwana. 23 5:23 1:22; 1 Akor 11:3Kwani mulume nde kitswa cha muchewe, dza viryahu Masihi arizho ndiye kitswa cha kanisa. Kanisa ndo mwiri wa Masihi na iye nde mwokoliwe. 24 Bai dza viryahu kanisa rimusikirazho Masihi, ache nao ni maasikire alume ao kahi za maut'u gosi.
25 5:25-28 1:23; Akol 3:19; 1 P'et 3:75:25 3:19Ninwi alume nanwi, ahendzeni ache enu, dza viryahu Masihi arizhorihendza kanisa, hatha akidzilazha kwa ut'uwe. 26 5:26 Arum 6:3-4; Tit 3:5; Aebu 10:22Naye wahenda vivi ili arithenge k'anda na arisafishe na madzi kukirira kwa neno ra Mulungu. 27 5:27 1:4; 2 Akor 11:2; Afil 1:10; Akol 1:22Bai Jesu wahenda vizho, ili haho andihokudzariimisha ro kanisa mbereze, rikale rinang'ala-ng'ala bila maramara, hedu minyau, hedu ut'u udziwohalana na gago. Ela rikale ni eri bila kaila kokosi. 28 Kwa ngira iyo bai, alume nao suthi maahendze ache ao, dza vizho maihendzazho miri yao enye. Mut'u ahendzaye muchewe, nikukala anadzihendza mwenye. 29 Kwani k'akuna mut'u adimaye kuumena mwiriwe, hat'u ha vizho iye ni kuurisa na kuutsundzat'o, dza viryahu Masihi arihenderazho Kanisa. 30 5:30 1:23; 1 Akor 12:27Mana riro ni mwiriwe, na siswi fu vilungo zha uwo mwiriwe. 31 5:31 Mwan 2:24“Kwa ut'u uu mut'u andamuricha babaye na mameye agwirane na muchewe, na ao airi mandakala mwiri mumwenga.” 32 5:32 Ugun 19:7Siri ii be ni bomu, ela ninanena dzulu za Masihi na Kanisa. 33 Kwa vizho, ninwi nanwi, kila mumwenga wenu naamuhendze muchewe dza vizho adzihendzazho ye mwenye. Muche naye niamuishimu mulumewe.
