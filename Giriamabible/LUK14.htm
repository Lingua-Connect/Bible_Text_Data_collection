14
Jesu anahoza mukongo siku ya Sabato
1 14:1-6 Mar 2:27-28Siku mwenga iriyokala ni Sabato, Jesu wakwendarya chakurya kahi za nyumba ya mut'u ariye were ni kahi za o abomu a Mafarisayo. Vikara o Mafarisayo makikala manamulola kala andahenda ut'u wani. 2 Na lola ho mbere za Jesu were hana mwanamulume ariyekala ana ukongo wa magulu na mikono kwadzala. 3 Jesu akiauza o alimu a Sheria na o Mafarisayo akiamba, “Vidze Sheria yehu inaruhusu kuhoza mut'u siku ya Sabato?” 4 O at'u hat'u ha kumudzigidzya o makimunyamalira zii. Kwa vizho Jesu akimugwira mukono ye mukongo akimuhoza gonya akimwamba ende mudzini. 5 14:5 13:15-16; Math' 12:11Gonya Jesu akiagalukira o alimu a Sheria na Mafarisayo, akiauza akiamba, “Ni h'ani hano ariye anadima kuricha ndzowewe hedu ng'ombeye idziyogwa ndani ya kisima asiyamboze kwa kukala ni siku ya Sabato?” 6 Nao k'amapatire ra kumudzigidzya bule.
Jesu anafundisha dzulu za kudzitsereza
7 14:7 20:46Jesu arihoona viryahu marizhokala o ajeni manawaniira vihi zha ishima ho chakuryani, waaadha kivivi, 8 14:8-10 Ndhum 25:7“Mut'u akikusunga harusi, usende ukakelesi zho vihi zha ishima p'ore hakakala were hadzasungwa mut'u aishimiwaye kukira uwe, 9 kwani ye mwenye mudzi adziyemusunga anadima akadza akaamba, ‘Hedu ungarara umuhise yuyu cho kihi,’ nawe ugwirwe ni haya ukakeresi zho vihi zha ts'ini. 10 Ela bai usungwaho harusi, enda ukakeresi zho vihi zha ts'ini ili iye adziyekusunga akikona haho anadima kuamba, ‘Musena, hinde ukakeresi kahi za vihi vidzo zaidhi hedu.’ Na vivi vindakup'a ishima mbere za o ajeni andziyo osi. 11 14:11 Math' 23:12Mana iye ahendzaye kudzanula andatserezwa, na iye adzitserezaye bai andanulwa.” 12 Gonya Jesu akimwamba ye mwenye mudzi, “Uikaho sherehe ya chakurya cha mutsana hedu cha dzulo, usiasunge asenao, hedu nduguzo, hedu mbarizo, hedu majiranio mashaha, kwa kukala ukihenda vizho, ao nao mandakusunga na vindakala ni here madziokuriha. 13 14:13 Kumbu 14:29Ela uusaho sherehe, bai asunge at'u akiya, vidende, virema, na atsowi a matso. 14 Ukihenda vizho undahaswa haho wakathi at'u a Mulungu mandihofufulwa, kwani ao akiya, vidende, virema na atsowi a matso k'amadima kukuriha bule, ela Mulungu anadima kukuriha.”
Fumbo ra sherehe bomu
Math'ayo 22:1-10
15 14:15 13:29Mumwenga kahi za ao mariokala haho arihosikira maneno gago wamwamba Jesu, “Be k'amana raha sana ao mandioirya yo sikuk'uu kahi za uthawali wa Mulungu!” 16 Ela Jesu akimwamba, “Kwereko na mut'u mumwenga ariyekala anamala kuusa sherehe bomu sana, naye akisunga at'u anji jeri-jeri.” 17 Bai wakathi irihokala yo haja i thayari kamare, yuyahu mut'u wahuma muhumikiwe akaambire o asungwa kukala maut'u be ga thayari. 18 Ela osi makimboza visingwa kukala k'amandadima kufika bule ko hajani. Ye wa kwandza akiamba, “Mut'u mimi dzagula hat'u na kwa zhozhosi suthi nende nikahalole, kwa vizho nashikithika sindadima kudza bule.” 19 Na wa hiri akiamba, “Mimi be dzagula ndzao kumi za kuhendera kazi hatha vivi unonazho be dzakala ninenda nikazijeze, kwa vizho nashikithika sindadima kudza bule.” 20 Na mungine ye akiamba, “Dzahala muche, kwa vizho ko kudza be ni vikomu.” 21 Ye muhumiki akiuya ko kwa bwanawe achendamwambira gosi marigo were o asungwa madzamwambira. Kusikira vizho, ye mwenye haja watsukirwa zhomu sana, akimwamba ye muhumikiwe, “Enda mara mwenga hatha kahi za wo mudzi na udze na o akiya, vidende, virema na hatha atsowi a matso mario kahi za barabara za wo mudzi na zho vigira-vigira kahi za go madzumba.” 22 Ye muhumiki akihenda vizho na arihouya akidzaamba, “Viryahu udzizhonihuma be dzavihenda, ela kuchere na nafasi.” 23 Bai ye mwenye yo sherehe akimwamba ye muhumiki, “Enda kuko mabarabarani na vigira zha magulu na ukaasitire at'u madze ili ii haja yangu idzale. 24 14:24 22:30Kwani namwambira kamare k'ahana hatha mumwenga kahi za ao nirioasunga sherehe chaho andekudzathatha kiki chakurya changu.”
Garama za kukala mwanafundzi wa Jesu
Math'ayo 10:37-38
25 Na arihokala Jesu akwendani, kwereko na kundi bomu ra at'u ririro were rinamuthuwa. Bai Jesu akiagalukira na akiambira, 26 14:26 12:51-53; 18:29; Math' 10:37“Mut'u yoyosi ahendzaye kunithuwa mimi, suthi anihendze mimi kukira viryahu aahendzazho babaye na mameye, muchewe na anae, nduguze na hatha ye mwenye, ndo akale mwanafundzi wangu. 27 14:27 9:23; Math' 16:24; Mar 8:34Na mut'u yoyosi ariye k'adima kuhala musalabawe na kunithuwa k'adima kukala mwanafundzi wangu. 28 Vidze, mumwenga wenu kala anamala kuaka munara, andaandza kuaka hasiho kukelesi na kugwaga kitswa amanye kala ana p'esa za kuthosha kuakira wo munara? 29 Kwani akidziakira hasiho kuaza anadima akakala k'andauivisha bule bada ya kuika wo musinji, na kila andekira na kuona zho virizho, andamutseka. 30 Kwani o at'u mandaamba, ‘H'aya, wadzihendesha kuaka vino zhamuremeza.’ 31 Na muthawali endaho na shikari alifu kumi, kupiga k'ondo muthawali mwandziwe ariye ana shikari alifu mirongo miri, jeri wenye iye ni kukelesi kwandza akaoja kala anadima kumudhima ye mwandziwe na o shikari alifu kumi. 32 Akiona kukala k'andamwadima, bai andahuma athumia mende makamone ye muthawali kabila k'adzafika hehi, ili madime kurya amani. 33 Bai kahi za ngira iyo nanwi, k'ahana adimaye kukala mwanafundzi wangu, asihoandza kuricha maut'u gosi arigo nago.”
Munyu na mulangaza
Math'ayo 5:13; Mariko 9:50
34 “Munyu ni mudzo, ela ukikala udzauya k'aukoza kaheri, k'ahana ngira idimayo kuuhendya ukakoza, 35 14:35 Math' 11:15hatha virizho haho ni kutsufwa thu, kwa kukala k'auna faidha kwa wo mutsanga, hedu hatha kwa mbolea. Mwenye masikiro, naasikire.”
