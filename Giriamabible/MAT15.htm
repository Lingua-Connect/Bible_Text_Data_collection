15
Maadha ga Mafarisayo
Mariko 7:1-13
1 Siku mwenga, Mafarisayo na alimu a Sheria, mambola Jerusalemu machenda hatha kuko arikokala Jesu. Manafika kuko makimwamba, 2 15:2 23:25; Luk 11:38-39“Mbona o anafundzio manaribanda ro adha ra kuoga mikono hurirorichirwa ni akare ehu? Kwani kala manarya k'amaoga mikono.” 3 Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Mbona nwi munabanda malagizo ga Mulungu ili muthuwe maadha genu enye? 4 15:4 Kumbo 20:12; 21:17; Kumbu 5:16Mulungu walagiza kukala, ‘Muishimu babayo na mameyo.’ Kidza akiamba, ‘Ye andemuhukana babaye hedu mameye, kumala kwalagwa naalagwe.’ 5 Ela nwi munafundisha kukala, mut'u akihala kit'u adimacho kumup'a babaye hedu mameye na akakala adzamwaviza, na hat'uhe akilazhe kwa Mulungu, 6 andakala k'ana haja ya kuaishimu bule. Mbona munarihenda neno ra Mulungu kutsakala na nguvu, kwa muthuwe maadha genu enye? 7 15:7-9 Isa 29:13Munadzihendya sana ninwi. Nabii Isaya wanenat'o sana dzulu zenu. Kwani waamba,
8 ‘At'u aa mananogoha kwa makanwa gao,
ela mioyo yao i kure nami.
9 Hatha makanivoya ni bule,
kwa kukala manahala maadha ga anadamu,
na kugafundisha here malagizo gangu.’ ”
Kimuhendyacho mut'u kukala mukolo
Mariko 7:14-23
10 Bai Jesu arihogonya kunena na o Mafarisayo, akiaiha o at'u akiambira, “Nisirikizani na mung'alirwe; 11 15:11 Mahe 10:15; 1 T'imo 4:4; Tit 1:15Changiracho kanwani mwa mut'u, k'akimuhendya mukolo. Kimuhendyacho mut'u akakala mukolo, ni kiryahu chambolacho kanwani mwakwe.”
12 K'uzhona zho marihombola haho, anafundzi makimwamba kukala, “Unamanya kukala virya udzizhovinena harya vidzaatsukiza o Mafarisayo?” 13 Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Kila mumera uriwo k'aulayirwe ni Baba ariye ko mulunguni, undang'olwa. 14 15:14 23:16, 24; Luk 6:39; Arum 2:19Aricheni, ao ni vipofu malongozao vipofu andziyao. Na kipofu akimulongoza kipofu mwandziwe, k'amafikahi bule, osi mandavoromoka winani.”
15 Ela P'et'ero akimwamba Jesu, “Fambire mana ya rirya fumbo hedu.” 16 Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Hatha ninwi nanwi k'amung'alirwa bule? 17 Samba ukirya kit'u kinenda ndanini mwako, gonya uchenda chandze unenda ukachamboze. 18 Ela maut'u mai gambolago kanwani nikukala gala moyoni, na gago ndo gamuhendyago mut'u kukala mukolo. 19 15:19 Agal 5:19-21Kwani moyo ndo udimao kumboza maazo mai gamuhendyago mut'u akalaga, akazinga, akaiya, akamboza ushahidhi wa ulongo, na akahukana mut'u. 20 Gaga ndo maut'u gamuhendyago mut'u kukala mukolo. Ela kurya bila kuoga mikono dza zho mahendzazho ao, k'avidima kumuhendya mut'u yoyosi akale mukolo.”
Kuluhiro bomu ra mwanamuche
Mariko 7:24-30
21 Bai Jesu akimbola jimbo ra Galilaya na achendakala kahi za eneo ra midzi ya Tiro na Sidoni. 22 15:22 20:30-31Arihofika kuko, lola mwanamuche mumwenga Muk'anani ariyekala akitoa p'ande zizo, akimuthuwa na akiluta mumiro akimwamba, “Nionera mbazi h'ewe Bwana, Mwana wa Daudi!*15:22 Mwana wa Daudi ni dzina ririrohumirwa kumwihira Masihi. Musichana wangu ana p'ep'o, be a vii-vii zhomu sana.” 23 Jesu k'amudzigidzyire kit'u. Bai o anafundzi makimwamba Jesu, “Bwana, mwambe ariche kuhuthuwa-thuwa, kwani be anahupigira londzo.” 24 15:24 10:6; Arum 15:8Jesu akiamba, “Mimi nahumwa kwa Aiziraeli h'akeye, ng'ondzi zirizongamika.” 25 Ela ye mwanamuche akidzamupigira mavindi akiamba, “Naviza Bwana.” 26 Jesu akimwamba, “Si karakara bule nihale chakurya cha ahoho nikitsuhire k'uro.” 27 Ye mwanamuche akidzigidzya akiamba, “U mujeri, ela hatha ye k'uro naye nikurya magande gadzigogwagwa ni ye mwenye k'uro.” 28 15:28 8:13Jesu akimwamba, “Mwanamuche uwe be una kuluhiro bomu sana. Bai vizho umalazho navikale.” Na wakathi uwo wenye ye musichanawe akihola kare.
Jesu anahoza akongo anji
29 15:29 5:1Vikara Jesu arihombola haho, wakira na k'anda-k'anda za ro ziya ra Galilaya, akipanda kuko dzulu virimani, achendakeresi kuko. 30 Bai at'u anji makidza na akongo ao, virema, vidende, vipofu, mabwibwi na akongo angine anji. Akongo aa makudzaikwa mbere za Jesu, naye akiahoza osini. 31 O at'u marihoona viryahu Jesu ahozazho akongo, machangalala na makimulika Mulungu wa Iziraeli. Maona mabwibwi gananena, at'u enye virema mananenda, vidende manahola na vipofu manaona.
Jesu anarisa at'u zaidhi ya alifu ne
Mariko 8:1-10
32 15:32-39 14:13-2115:32 9:36Vikara Jesu akiaiha anafundzie akiamba, “At'u aa be mananishononesa zhomu. Hangu makale nami haha, rero ni hahu, na vivi k'amana ut'u wowosi wa kurya, nami ts'endzi niafumule na ndzala, p'ore makendagwa na mutsare mo ngirani.” 33 K'uzhona o anafundzie makimuza makimwamba, “Bwana, cho chakurya cha kuarisa at'u ano osi hano nyika hundachambozahi?” 34 Jesu akiauza akiamba, “Kwani muna mikahe mingahi?” Makiamba, “Mifungahe, na vikumba zha kuthala.” 35 Bai Jesu akiambira o at'u makelesi ho ts'i. 36 Marihogonya kukelesi, Jesu akihala yo mikahe mifungahe na zho vikumba, akilazha muv̱era gonya akivimogola-mogola akiap'a o anafundzie maagazhire o at'u. 37 Lola at'u osini marya vivii hatha makivilola, kidza o anafundzi makidzaza k'ahu fungahe za visiku-visiku virizhokala vidzasala. 38 Na murongo wa o at'u mariorya were unafika alume alifu ne, bila kuthala ache na ahoho. 39 Bai Jesu arihokala adzagonya kuafumula o at'u, wakwera dau achenda jimbo ra Magadani.
