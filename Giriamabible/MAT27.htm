27
Jesu anahirikwa kwa P'ilato
Mariko 15:1; Luka 23:1-2; Johana 18:28-32
1 Kurihocha, o alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi mathunganana kaheri makipanga viryahu mandizhohenda hatha mamwalage Jesu. 2 Bai mamuhala makimufunga, na makimuhirika kwa luwali P'ilato.
Juda anadzifunga lugwe
Mahendo 1:18-19
3 27:3 26:15Juda arihoona kukala ro Baraza ratosa kukala mamwalage Jesu, wadziutha sana, hatha akihala zho visiku mirongo mihahu zha fedha achenda nazho kuko kwa alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi ili akaaujire. 4 Arihofika kuko waagololeza akiamba, “Nahenda dambi bomu kumusaliti mut'u asiye dambi na mut'u.” O makimwamba, “Gago be ni gako gago, sino k'ahugamanya.” 5 Naye kusikira vizho, akihala zho visiku zha fedha akivitsuha mo Hekaluni, achambola achendadzifunga lugwe.
6 27:6 Kumbu 23:18Vikara o alombi abomu makivitsola-tsola viryahu visiku zha fedha gonya makiamba, “P'esa zizi be zina kilatso, na Sheria yehu k'aihuruhusu kuika p'esa dza zizi kahi za akiba ya mumu Hekaluni.” 7 Kwa vizho bada ya kuojana-ojana makivihala machendagula hat'u harihokala hakiifwa Munda wa Muumbi wa Nyungu, hakale hat'u ha kuzikwa ajeni. 8 Na kulaiza siku iyo hatha rero munda uwo unaifwa Munda wa Milatso.
9 27:9-10 Zaka 11:12-13; Jere 32:8-9Kwa ut'u uwo wenye bai, garyahu maneno garigonenwa ni nabii Jeremia gakithimira. Kwani Jeremia wanena akiamba, “Mahala vizho visiku mirongo mihahu zha fedha, iriyo were ndo bei ya kugulira mut'u iriyoikwa ni o ana a Iziraeli, 10 makivigula wo Munda wa Muumbi wa Nyungu here Bwana arizhonilagiza.”
Jesu anauzwa-uzwa maswali ni P'ilato
Mariko 15:2-5; Luka 23:3-5; Johana 18:33-38
11 27:11 2:2; 27:29, 37, 42; Mar 15:2, 9, 12, 18, 26, 32; Luk 23:3, 37-38; Joh 1:49; 12:13; 18:33, 39; 19:3, 15, 19, 21Vikara Jesu arihohirikwa kwa P'ilato, waimishwa haho mbereze na P'ilato akimuza akiamba, “Vidze we ndiwe muthawali wa Ayahudi?” Jesu akimudzigidzya akimwamba, “Uwe udzanena.” 12 27:12-14 Joh 19:9; Math' 26:63Ela o alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi marihomushithaki, Jesu wanyamala zii kamare. 13 P'ilato arihoona viryahu, akimuza Jesu akiamba, “Go maut'u manji genye madzigoamba wagahenda unagasikira?” 14 Ela Jesu ye akithuwa kunyamala vizho bila kudzigidzya hatha ango mwenga, hatha ye P'ilato vikimwangalaza zhomu zhenye.
Jesu anahukumiwa kwalagwa
Mariko 15:6-15; Luka 23:13-26; Johana 18:39–19:16
15 Kila sikuk'uu ya P'asaka, P'ilato were ana mazowera ga kuambozera Ayahudi mufungwa mumwenga mandiyemutsagula enye. 16 Bai kahi za wakathi uwo, kwere kuna mufungwa ariyekala adzaluta nguma, dzinare ni Baraba. 17 Kwa vizho o Ayahudi marihokundzumanyikana hamwenga, P'ilato akiauza akiamba, “Nimwambozere Baraba hedu nimwambozere Jesu aifwaye Masihi?” 18 Kwani P'ilato wamanya kukala o athumia a Kiyahudi madzamushithaki Jesu kwa sababu ya wivu thu.
19 27:19 Luk 23:47Bai P'ilato arihokala anakeresi kahi za kihiche cha hukumu alamule, muchewe akilagiza uv̱oro akiamba, “Maut'u gago be usigangirire kamare, kwani mut'u yuyu be ni muhachi. Mana usiku nidzaloha ndoso mbii-mbii sana dzulu za kwakwe. Nayo ndoso iyo idzanisirinya.” 20 27:20-23 Mahe 3:13-14Ela o alombi abomu na o athumia a Kiyahudi makiangola-ngola o at'u kukala maambe mambozerwe Baraba, ela Jesu ye alagwe. 21 Kwa vizho P'ilato arihouza kaheri na kuamba, “Kahi za ano at'u airi munahendza nimwambozere h'ani?” O at'u makiamba, “Baraba.” 22 P'ilato akiauza akiamba, “Na yuno Jesu aifwaye Masihi ye nimuhendedze?” Nao makinena kwa umwenga makiamba, “Naasulubiwe!” 23 P'ilato akiauza kaheri akiamba, “Kwani adzakosani kibomu?” Ela o hatha makikaza kupiga k'ululu makiamba, “Naasulubiwe!” 24 27:24 Kumbu 21:6-9; Zabu 26:6Bai P'ilato arihoona kukala hatha akaenderera kunena nao kidza k'avindafwaha kit'u na kidza o at'u were madzakwacha kuhenda fujo, akihala madzi akioga mikono mbere za ro kundi ra at'u, akiamba, “Milatso ya mut'u yuyu k'aidzulu yangu bule, i dzulu zenu.” 25 27:25 Mahe 5:28Na lola o at'u makidzigidzya kwa umwenga makiamba, “Milatsoye naikale dzulu zehu siswi na dzulu za ana ehu.” 26 27:26-31 20:19Kwa vizho P'ilato akiambozera Baraba, gonya akiamuru Jesu apigwe vikotho, ndo alazhwe akasulubiwe.
Shikari manamuv̱udhya Jesu
Mariko 15:16-21; Johana 19:2-3
27 Jesu arihokala adzalazhwa akasulubiwe, o shikari a luwali P'ilato mamuhala machangira naye mumo nyumbani mwa luwali, na cho kikosi chosi cha shikari kikimudengereka. 28 27:28 Luk 23:11Bai mamuhala makimuvula nguwo, gonya makimuvika k'andzu th'une. 29 27:29 27:11Manambola haho makitengeza kiremba cha miya makimuvika. Gonya makihala kidhath'a cha mwandzi makimugwiza kahi za wo mukonowe wa kurya. Bai marihogonya vizho makikwacha kupiga mavindi na kumuv̱udhya Jesu na kumwamba, “K'una ut'u muheshimiwa muthawali wa Ayahudi?” 30 27:30 26:67Bai mamutsuhira mahe, gonya makimunyagula cho kidhath'a makimupiga nacho mo kitswani. 31 Marihokala madzamuv̱udhya zha kuthosha, mamuvula yo k'andzu th'une, makimuvika zo nguwoze kaheri, makimuthongozha kwendamusulubisha.
Jesu anasulubiwa
Mariko 15:21-32; Luka 23:27-43; Johana 19:17-27
32 Vikara marihokala madzambola kwa P'ilato makwendani, mumo ngirani mathungana mwanamulume mumwenga Muk'irene aifwaye Simoni. Bai o shikari makimulazimisha autsukule wo musalaba wa Jesu. 33 Bai makwenda vivii hatha hat'u haifwaho Gologoth'a, yani Ribeshe ra Kitswa. 34 Makimup'a Jesu divai ya kutsanganywa na ut'u wa utsungu sana, ela arihoithatha akikahala kuinwa.
35 27:35 Zabu 22:18Marihokala madzagonya kumusulubisha, mahala zo nguwoze makizigazha kwa kuhenda kuzipigira k'ura. 36 Gonya makikelesi haho kumurinda. 37 27:37 27:11Dzulu za cho kitswa cha Jesu haikwa kibao kirichoorwa kosa mariromushithakira. Nacho chere kinaamba vivi: Yuyu Ni Jesu, Muthawali Wa Ayahudi.
38 27:38 Isa 53:12Jesu wasulubiwa hamwenga na aivi airi. Mumwenga mukono wa kurya, na mungine mukono wa kumots'o.
At'u manamuv̱udhya Jesu
39 27:39 Zabu 22:7; 109:25At'u mariokira na haho arihosulubiwa Jesu, mamusholera, makithukusa vitswa zhao kikav̱udhye, 40 27:40 26:61, 63makiamba, “Kw'aamba undaribanda ro Hekalu na uriake kwa siku t'ahu vino? H'aya dziokole mwenye be vivi. Chamba u Mwana wa Mulungu jeri, thima kula ho musalabani.” 41 Na lola o alombi abomu, na alimu a Sheria, na athumia a Kiyahudi nao makimusholera dza vizho zha akizi a ngira. Makiamba, “Waokola at'u angine, ela vino k'adima kudzakola mwenye. 42 27:42 27:11Samba ni muthawali wa Iziraeli ye, naathime kula ho musalabani, naswi fundamuamini. 43 27:43 Zabu 22:8; Math' 26:63Amba ye were anamukuluhira Mulungu sana; hatha anaamba kukala ni Mwana wa Mulungu. H'aya kala ye Mulungu anamuhendza jeri, be vivi naamwakole.” 44 Hatha aryahu aivi airi mariosulubiwa hamwenga naye kidza nao mamusholera dza vizho zha o angine.
Jesu anamarigizika
Mariko 15:33-41; Luka 23:44-49; Johana 19:28-30
45 27:45 Amo 8:9Bada ya Jesu kusulubiwa, ts'i yosi yagit'ana kiza kuandzira saa sitha muhadhi saa chenda. 46 27:46 Zabu 22:1Inafika mwendo wa saa chenda Jesu akirira kwa sauthi bomu akiamba, “Eloi Eloi, lama sabakithani?” Yani, “Mulungu wangu, Mulungu wangu, mbona udzaniricha?” 47 Kusikira vizho, angine kahi za ao at'u mariokala maimire haho makiamba, “Sirikizani, anamwiha Elija benwi!” 48 27:48 Zabu 69:21Na haho na haho, mumwenga wao akikot'a malo achendavwika buwa kahi za siki, akiridunga mudhath'ani akimup'a Jesu anyunye. 49 Ela andziye makimwamba, “Richa hulole chamba Elija andakudzamuthizha.” [Na mumwenga wao akihala fumo akimudunga lubavuni, akimbola milatso na madzi.]*27:49 Kuna zhuwo vingine virizho k'avina ii sehemu ii ya kifungu. 50 Bai Jesu akipiga k'ululu kaheri na akitoka roho. 51 27:51 Kumbo 26:3; Aebu 10:19-20Na lola yo p'azia kahi za ro Hekalu yapashuka ikigazhika p'ande mbiri, kula dzulu hatha ts'ini. Ts'i ikithethema na mawe gakibandika-bandika. 52 Mbira nyinji za at'u a Mulungu zikigunuka na o at'u enye makifufuka, 53 na machambola mo mbirani. Na lola Jesu arihofufuka, at'u aa mangira kahi za mudzi mueri wa Jerusalemu na makionewa ni at'u anji enye.
54 27:54 14:33; 26:63; Mar 15:39Bai o shikari mariokala manamurinda Jesu, hamwenga na ye mubomu wao marihoona zho ts'i irizhokala inathethema, hamwenga na go maut'u mangine gosi, mangirwa ni woga sana hatha makiamba, “Yuyu were ni Mwana wa Mulungu jeri.”
55 Kwere kuna anaache mariomuthuwa Jesu hangu Galilaya. Anaache aa ndo mariokala makimwaviza Jesu na mahitaji ga kwakwe. Bai makala maimire kure vichache kulolera kila kirichokala. 56 Kahi zao were hana Maryamu Magidalena, Maryamu mameye Jakobu na Josefu, na mameye Jakobu na Johana, ana a Zebedayo.
Kuzikwa kwa Jesu
Mariko 15:42-47; Luka 23:50-56; Johana 19:38-42
57 27:57-58 Kumbu 21:22-23Kurihokala kunakala kidziloni, kukidza mwanamulume mumwenga wa kula Arimath'ea ariyeifwa Josefu. Mwanamulume yuyu were naye ni mwanafundzi wa Jesu na were ni thajiri. 58 Bai wakwenda hatha kwa P'ilato achendamuvoya P'ilato amup'e mwiri wa Jesu akauzike. Naye P'ilato akilagiza agerwe. 59 Kwa vizho Josefu akiuhala akiulinga-linga ts'andza ya kitani safi, 60 na achendauzika kahi za mbiraye mwenye, ariyokala waitsonga iweni. Mbira ii were ni mbisha yenye hatha were k'aidzazikwa mut'u. Bai wamwangiza mumo na akisukuma ro iwe akifunga, gonya akihauka. 61 Wakathi uwo Josefu arihokala anamuzika Jesu, Maryamu Magidalena na ye Maryamu mungine were makelesi kulolana na iyo mbira manalolera.
Shikari manahumwa makarinde mbira ya Jesu
62 Siku ya hiriye, yani yo siku iriyothuwana na yo Siku ya Mathayarisho, o alombi abomu na Mafarisayo makwendathunganana na P'ilato. 63 27:63 12:40Makimwamba, “Muheshimiwa, at'u siswi be fudzakumbukira ut'u umwenga siswi. Yuyahu mulongo arihokala achere moyo, waamba kukala, bada ya siku t'ahu andafufuka. 64 27:64 28:13Kwa vizho bai, lagiza shikari makarinde yo mbira muhaka yo siku ya hahu. Kwani kala k'ahundahenda vizho, o anafundzi a kwakwe manadima kudzamwiya gonya makaambira at'u kukala adzafufuka. Na ulongo uwo undakala mui zhomu hatha uwo wa kwandza mbai.”
65 P'ilato akiamba, “Shikari munao, kwa vizho hakikishani kukala inarindwa zha kuthosha.” 66 Kwa vizho machendahala ro iwe ra ho muryangoni makiripiga muhuri. Gonya makiika shikari marinde.
