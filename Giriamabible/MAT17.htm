17
Jesu anang'ala-ng'ala
Mariko 9:2-13; Luka 9:28-36
1 17:1 5:1Vikara siku t'andahu bada ya kunena dzulu za kudzakwe, Jesu wamuhala P'et'ero, na Jakobu, na Johana nduguye Jakobu, na akikwera nao dzulu za murima mure, kure na at'u. 2 17:2-8 2 P'et 1:16-18; Ugun 1:16Bai marihokala ma kuko, Jesu akigaluka sura kamare kuno manalolera. Usowe ukikala unang'ala-ng'ala here dzuwa na nguwoze zikikala nyaruhe tse-tse-tse. 3 Na lola haho henye, Musa na Elija makiambolera makikala mananena naye. 4 Bai P'et'ero arihoona viryahu akimwamba Jesu, “Bwana, ni vidzo zho zha fu haha. Ukihendza nadima kumuakira vibanda vihahu mino. Kimwenga chako, kimwenga cha Musa, na kimwenga cha Elija.” 5 17:5 3:17Lola P'et'ero were k'adzatosa, kuchambola ingu ra kung'ala rikidzaafinikira. Na sauthi ikimbola mo inguni ikiamba, “Yuyu nde Mwanangu muhendzwi anifwahizaye, musirikizeni iye.” 6 K'ona o anafundzi marihosikira vizho machangirwa ni woga zhomu makigwa na moyo. 7 Ela Jesu akidzaagut'a akiamba, “Musigohe bule, ukani.” 8 Nao marihotsuha matso gao makimona Jesu thu h'akeye, k'amaonere mut'u mungine bule.
9 17:9 16:20Badaye marihokala manatharamuka kula ko dzulu ya wo murima, Jesu akiakokot'era o anafundzie akiamba, “Viryahu mudzizhozhona kuryahu, be musizhambire mut'u kamare, muhaka haho Mwana wa Mudamu andihofufulwa.” 10 17:10-11 Mala 4:5-6; Math' 16:14O anafundzi makimuza Jesu makiamba, “Mbona o alimu a Sheria manaamba ni lazima Elija adze kwandza?” 11 Akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Zho ni vizho, Elija ni lazima athangulie kuthayarisha kila kit'u. 12 17:12-13 11:14; Luk 1:17; Joh 1:21Ela namwambira kukala, Elija wakudza kare, ela at'u k'amamanyire ndeye bule, makimuhenda kila marichoona ni karakara. Na vizho zhenye ndizho mandizhomutesa Mwana wa Mudamu.” 13 Jesu arihogonya kunena, o anafundzi makimanya kukala were ananena dzulu za Johana Mubat'izaji.
Jesu anamboza muvulana p'ep'o
Mariko 9:14-29; Luka 9:37-43
14 Vikara marihouya ko at'u marikokala, mwanamulume mumwenga akimudhana Jesu na akidzapiga mavindi ho mbereze 15 akiamba, “Bwana, muthana wangu ana vitsala vikali na vinamup'a thabu sana. Mara nyinji nikumugwaga mohoni na hatha madzini. Kwa vizho muonere mbazi Bwana. 16 Nidzaarehera aryahu anafundzio mamuhoze ela k'amazhadimire bule.” 17 17:17 Kumbu 32:5, 20K'uzhona Jesu arihosikira viryahu akidzigidzya akiamba, “H'enwi at'u a kizhazi cha kwangamika ngira, musio na kuluhiro, nindakala nanwi hatha rini? Nindamuvumirira hatha rini? Murehe kuno ye muhoho.” 18 Naye arihorefwa, Jesu akimuchemera yuyahu p'ep'o wa vitsala akimwambola, na ye muhoho akihola kare. 19 Vikara badaye, o anafundzi makimuthuwa Jesu kinjama machendamuza makiamba, “Bwana, kwanoni sino k'ahudimire kumwamboza ye p'ep'o?” 20 17:20 13:31; 14:31; 21:21; Mar 11:23; Luk 17:6Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Kuluhiro renu ni thithe ndosa k'amuzhadimire. Ela namwambira ujeri siku mundihokala na kuluhiro thithe renye here katembe ka haradali, mundadima kuwambira uu murima uhauke wende kut'u kungine, nawo undakwenda. K'akuna kit'u kindichokala k'achadimikika. [ 21 Ela p'ep'o dza yuyu k'ambola rahisi-rahisi bule, isihokala kwa kufunga na kuvoya.”]
Jesu anathala kaheri dzulu za kifwache
Mariko 9:30-32; Luka 9:43-45
22 17:22-23 16:21Bai siku mwenga, marihokala madzakundzumanyikana hamwenga ko Galilaya, Jesu akiambira o anafundzie akiamba, “Suthi Mwana wa Mudamu alazhwe mikononi mwa at'u. 23 Nao mandamwalaga, ela siku ya hahu andafufuka.” Marihosikira vizho, o anafundzi mashononeka sana.
Jesu anariha kodi ra Hekalu
24 17:24 Kumbo 30:13Vikara marihofika mudzi wa Kaperinaumu, athozi a kodi ra Hekalu makimuthuwa P'et'ero makimuza, “Yuno mwalimu wenu kisha nikuriha kodi ra Hekalu?” 25 P'et'ero akiamba, “Ee nikuriha.” Gonya thiye achenda ko nyumbani kwendamwambira Jesu. Ela kabila P'et'ero k'adzanena, Jesu akimuza swali akiamba, “Simoni kuno dhuniani, athawali nikuthoza kodi at'u a kwao enye, hedu at'u a kula ts'i nyingine?” 26 P'et'ero akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Mathoza at'u a ts'i nyingine.” Jesu akiamba, “Kwa vizho at'u a kwao ma huru. 27 Ela kwa kukala k'afwendzi kuaudhi, enda vivi hatha ko ziyani ukatsuhe kiloo. Ro kumba ra kwandza undirorigwira rigunule mo kanwani undaona shekeli mwenga, ihale yo shekeli udze nayo urihe kodi rangu na rako.”
