12
P'aulo ananena dzulu za nema ariyojaliwa ni Mulungu
1 Nindadzionya, ingahokala kudzionya k'akuna faidha yoyosi. Ela vivi, nindanena dzulu za maono na ugunulo arionanyesa Bwana. 2 Ninamumanya Mumasihia mumwenga, ariyenulwa hatha sehemu ya hahu ya mulunguni miaka kumi na mine idziyokira. Kala were a kahi za mwiri, hedu kala were a kahi za Roho mino sigamanya, Mulungu nde amanyaye. 3 Mut'u iye ninamumanya, ela kala were a kahi za mwiri hedu kahi za Roho, mino sigamanya, Mulungu nde amanyaye. 4 Mut'u iye wanulwa hatha p'ehoni, achendasikira maneno maeri arigo mudamu k'ana ruhusa ya kuganena. 5 12:5-6 11:16, 30; 12:1; 1 Akor 3:21Dzulu za mut'u dza iye ninadima kudzionya. Ela mimi mwenye kare sidima kudzionya, isihokala kwa unyonge wangu thu. 6 Hatha nikakala nahendza kudzionya kidza si muzuzu bule mana nindakala ninanena wo ujeri. Ela kwa kukala ts'endzi mut'u yoyosi anihalire zaidhi ya zho anonazho hedu anisikirazho, sindadzionya bule. 7 12:7 Agal 3:13-14; Ayub 2:6Ela ili kwamba gaga maut'u mabomu nirigogunulirwa gasinihendye nikamera kinundu, nagwizwa mwiya kahi za mwiri wangu. Mwiya uu ni mujumbe wa Shethani ili kwamba anisirinye p'ore nikadzanula zhomu. 8 Namuvoya Bwana k'ana t'ahu mwiya uu unambole, 9 12:9 Isa 40:29-31; Arum 5:2; Afil 4:13ela akinamba kukala, “Nema yangu inakuthosha, mana nguvu zangu zikamilika kahi za unyonge.” Kwa vizho ninafwahirwa zhomu ni kudzionyera unyonge wangu, ili kwamba nguvu za Masihi zikakale dzulu zangu. 10 Kwa vizho ni radhi kukala munyonge, kuhukanwa, kupata thabu, kuteswa, na kupata mashaka kwa ut'u wa Masihi. Mana nikikala ni munyonge ndo nikukala nina nguvu.
P'aulo ananena dzulu za mahendzo ga kwakwe kwa Amasihia
11 Mimi be nidzakala muzuzu, na murionilazimisha nikale muzuzu ni ninwi. Yo karakara be were ni ninwi munilike ela nwi k'amuvihendere. Ee mino si kit'u kamwe, ela o ahumwi enu abomu manishinda na noni? 12 12:12 Mahe 13:6-12; 14:8-10; 16:16-18; 19:11-16; Arum 15:19Miv̱ano ya kunyesa kukala mimi ni muhumwi jeri yahendeka kahikahi zenu kahi za kuvumirira kosi. Miv̱ano ii yanyeswa kwa ngira ya miujiza, na kwa maut'u mangine garigohendeka kwa nguvu za Mulungu. 13 12:13 11:7-9Ut'u umwenga h'akeye, nirio simuhenderere ela makanisa mangine nagahendera, ni kumutegemea kwa mahitaji gangu. Nisamehani kwa kosa riro. 14 12:14 13:1Vivi ni thayari kamare kudza ko kwenu mara ya hahu. Na ts'endzi nidze nikale muzigo kwenu, mana simala mali zenu ela ninamumala ninwi enye. Kwani ahoho k'amav̱irya kuaikira akiba azhazi ao, ela azhazi ndo mav̱iryao kuaikira akiba ahoho ao. 15 12:15 1 Akor 16:24Ndo mimi nindafwahirwa zhomu kuhumika mimi mwenye, na kuhumira vit'u zhangu kwa ut'u wa roho zenu. Vidze nikikala ninamuhendza zhomu vino, mino siv̱irya kuhendzwa dza vizho nami? 16 H'aya navikale kukala sikalire muzigo kwenu, ela namupata kwa kumukenga na wo ulachu niriwo nawo here muambazho. 17 12:17 7:2Vidze ni h'ani niriyemuhuma adze amuhoke vit'u zhenu anirehere? 18 12:18 2:13; 8:6, 16-17Nirihomwamba Tito adze amulamuse, namugwizanya iye na ndugu mungine makidza. Na nina hakika kukala Tito k'amuhokere chochosi bule. Mana iye fwanenda kahi za Roho mumwenga, na mwendo wehu nawo were ni mumwenga.
19 Hangine munadima kuona kukala wakathi uu wosi fudzakala fukidzihehera kwenu. Hatha bule! Maut'u gaga huganenago hunaganena kahi za Masihi na mbere za Mulungu. Na kila kit'u funakihenda kwa kumujenga ninwi ahendzwi. 20 12:20 1:23; 1 Akor 1:10-11Ninagoha k'ai nikidza kwenu nindakudza nimuthekeze kahi za hali iriyo k'aindanifwahiza, na hangine ninwi nanwi k'amundafwahizwa ni zho nindizhoihalira hali iyo. Ninagoha kukala hangine nadima kudzathekeza kunenezana, kidzitso, kutsukizana, kudzihendza enye, kunenana vii, nyambo, kudzionya na fujo. 21 12:21 13:2Ninagoha kukala nindihokudza kaheri kuko kwenu, Mulungu wangu andanitharamusha mbere zenu na niaririre anji a aryahu mariohenda ukolo, na uzindzi, na th'amaa mbii hangu kapindi, na hatha vivi kidza k'amadzathubu bule.
