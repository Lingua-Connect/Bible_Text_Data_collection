26
Jesu anapangirwa njama ya kwalagwa
Mariko 14:1-2; Luka 22:1-2; Johana 11:45-53
1 26:1 7:28Vikara Jesu arihokala adzagonya kunena maneno gaga gosi, waambira anafundzie, 2 26:2 16:21“Here mumanyazho, muhondo ni sikuk'uu ya P'asaka. Na Mwana wa Mudamu andalazhwa ili asulubiwe.”
3 Wakathi uwo, alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi mathunganana nyumbani kwa Kayafa. Mut'u yuyu Kayafa were nde Mulombi mubomu mwenye wakathi uwo. 4 26:4-5 21:46Bai at'u ao mathunganana kuko ili mamale ngira ya kilachu ya kumugwirira Jesu na mamwalage. 5 Ela marihothunganana, mapatana kukala masimugwire Jesu wakathi wa P'asaka bule, p'ore at'u makathinya bugwa.
Jesu anamwagirwa mafuha ga marashi
Mariko 14:3-9; Johana 12:1-8
6 26:6-7 Luk 7:37-38Bai siku mwenga, Jesu wakwenda nyumbani kwa mwanamulume mumwenga ariyekala akiifwa Simoni Mahana. Mwanamulume yuyu were anatoa kahi za kadzidzi ka Beth'ania. 7 Bai arihokala haho, mwanamuche mumwenga akidza na th'up'a iriyokala i thele mafuha ga marashi kidza ga gali sana. Th'up'a ii yere yatengezwa na iwe ra alabasita. Bai arihofika haho, akimumwagira Jesu go mafuha mo kitswani kuno Jesu mwenye anahumira chakurya. 8 Bai o anafundzi marihoona viryahu, k'aviafwahire bule, maamba, “Kuno be tha si kwanonga kuno? 9 Mafuha gaga kala gadzaguzwa gakambola p'esa nyinji zenye na makagerwa masikini?”
10 Jesu arihovimanya akiamba, “Mbona munamubudzya ye mwanamuchengwa? Iye adzanihendera kit'u kidzo. 11 26:11 Kumbu 15:11Masikini munao siku zosini, ela mimi k'amundakala nami siku zosini bule. 12 Ut'u uu wa kunimwagira mafuha, yuyu mwanamuche adzauhenda ili anithayarishe kwa kuzikwa kwangu. 13 Nami namwambira ujeri, zho adzizhohenda mwanamuche yuyu vivi rero, nazho vindathalwa na kukumbukirwa kila undihohubiriwa wo Uv̱oro Udzo kahi za urumwengu muzima.”
Juda Isikariote anapanga kumusaliti Jesu
Mariko 14:10-11; Luka 22:3-6
14 Bai wakathi uwo, mumwenga wa aryahu anafundzi kumi na airi a Jesu aifwaye Juda Isikariote, wauka achenda kwa alombi abomu. 15 26:15 Kumbo 21:32; Zaka 11:12; Joh 11:57; 13:2Anafika kuko akiamba, “Vidze mut'u yuno nikimulazha kwenu, mundanip'ani?” K'ona arihonena vizho, aryahu alombi abomu makimuthalira visiku mirongo mihahu zha fedha makimup'a. 16 Na kulaizira haho, Juda akikala anamala nafasi mbidzo ya kumusalitira Jesu.
Jesu anarya P'asaka na anafundzie
Mariko 14:12-21; Luka 22:7-13; Johana 13:21-30
17 26:17-29 Kumbo 12:1-20Vikara siku ya kwandza ya sikuk'uu ya Mikahe Iso Ts'ats'u, anafundzi makwendamuza Jesu makiamba, “P'asaka ino undairyahi, fukakuthayarishire?” 18 Jesu akidzigidzya akiamba, “Ngirani wo mudzi wa Jerusalemu mwende hatha kwa yuyu mut'u yuyu. Mukifika kuko mwambeni kukala, ‘Mwalimu anaamba wakathi wangu u hehi, kwa vizho nindarya P'asaka na anafundzi angu kwako nyumbani.’ ” 19 Bai o anafundzi makihenda here marizhokala madzalagizwa ni Jesu. Manafika kuko makithayarisha yo P'asaka.
20 26:20-25 Joh 13:2, 21-30Kurihofika dziloni, Jesu na anafundzie kumi na airi makelesi marye chakurya. 21 Marihokala makuryani Jesu akinena akiamba, “Ninamwambira ujeri wenye kukala mumwenga wenu haha andanisaliti.” 22 O anafundzi marihosikira vizho makishikithika sana na makikwacha kumuza Jesu mumwenga-mumwenga makiamba, “Vidze ni mimi, Bwana?” 23 26:23 Zabu 41:9Ela Jesu akidzigidzya akiamba, “Iye atsotsaye nami kahi za muvure mumwenga nde andiyenisaliti. 24 26:24 Zabu 22:6-7, 15-18; Isa 53:9Mwana wa Mudamu anenda kare kamwe here Maoro gaambazho, ela shauriye iye mut'u amusalitiye! Vingakala baha kala k'azhalirwe.” 25 Vikara Jesu arihonena viryahu, ye musaliti wa kwakwe naye akiuza akiamba, “Vidze ni mimi Mwalimu?” Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Udzanena.”
26 26:26-28 1 Akor 10:16Bai marihokala makuryani, Jesu wahala mukahe, akimboza muv̱era kwa Mulungu, gonya akiumogola-mogola akiap'a o anafundzie akiamba, “Halani murye, uu ndo mwiri wangu.” 27 Na arihogonya kuap'a wo mukahe, akihala cho kikombe cha divai, akimboza muv̱era kwa Mulungu, gonya akiap'a o anafundzie akiamba, “Halani kikombe kiki mutsumbulirane, 28 26:28 Kumbo 24:8; Jere 31:31; Aebu 7:22; 9:15-20kwani ii ndo milatso yangu ya cho kilagane, imwagwayo ili at'u anji madime kusamehewa dambi zao. 29 Nami namwambira ujeri, sindanwa divai ii kaheri muhaka siku iyo nindihonwa divai mbisha hamwenga nanwi kahi za uthawali wa Baba.”
30 26:30 Luk 22:39; Joh 18:1Gonya makiimba wira na makiuka kwenda Murima wa Mizeituni.
Jesu ananena kimbere dzulu za P'et'ero andizhomukana
Mariko 14:27-31; Luka 22:31-34; Johana 13:36-38
31 26:31 Zaka 13:7; Math' 26:56; Joh 16:32Jesu akiambira kukala, “Usiku wa rero, ninwi nyosi mundaniricha h'akiyangu. Kwani Maoro ganaamba, ‘Nindamupiga ye murisa na zo ng'ondzi zosi zindatsamukana.’ 32 26:32 28:7, 16Ela haho nikikala nidzafufulwa, nindamuthangulia kwenda Galilaya.” 33 P'et'ero akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Hatha kala osi mandakuricha, mimi sindakuricha ng'o.” 34 26:34-35 Luk 22:33-34; Joh 13:36-3826:34 26:75Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Nakwambira ujeri, usiku wa rero kabila jogolo k'aridzaika, undanikana k'ana t'ahu.” 35 P'et'ero akimwamba Jesu, “Hatha kala ni kufwa nanifwe, ela sindakukana ng'o!” Na o anafundzi angine nao makinena dza vizho.
Jesu anavoya ko Geth'isemane
Mariko 14:32-42; Luka 22:39-46
36 26:36-46 Luk 5:16; Aebu 5:7-8Gonya Jesu na anafundzie makwenda hat'u haifwaho Geth'isemane. Marihofika kuko, akiambira o anafundzie akiamba, “Kelesini haha munitharize nami nasongera mbere vichache nikavoye.” 37 Arihokala akwendani akimuhala P'et'ero na o ana airi a Zebedayo, Jakobu na Johana achenda nao. Naye wakathi uwo washononeka zhomu na kubudzyika moyo. 38 Bai makwenda hatha manafika ko mbere, akiambira kukala, “Ninashononeka zhomu moyoni mwangu hatha ni here nindiyefwa. Bai kalani haha muchese nami.”
39 26:39 6:10; 20:22; Joh 18:11; Aebu 10:9Gonya Jesu akiaricha ano P'et'ero haho na akisongera mbere vichache. Achendagwa na moyo akivoya akiamba, “Baba, chamba vinadimikika mateso gaga naganiukire, gasinipate bule. Ela navikale here uhendzazho uwe, visikale here nihendzazho mimi bule.” 40 Kula haho akiauyira o anafundzie, naye akidzaaona marere. Akimwamba P'et'ero, “Yani k'amudimire kuchesa nami hatha ngaa saa mwenga? 41 26:41 Aefe 6:18; Aebu 2:18Chesani na muvoye sidze mukangira majezoni, kwani roho inahendza kamare, ela mwiri ni munyonge.”
42 Naye arihogonya kunena, akiaricha haho kaheri na achendavoya dza vizho zha kwandza akiamba, “Baba, chamba vinadimikika, mateso gaga gasinipate bule. Ela visikale here nihendzazho mimi, navikale here uhendzazho uwe.” 43 Arihogonya kuvoya vizho, akiauyira o ano P'et'ero. Anafika kuko, achendaathekeza marere kaheri. K'amadimire kukala matso kwa wo uts'indzizi mariwokala nawo.
44 26:44 2 Akor 12:8Bai Jesu akiaricha kaheri, na achendavoya mara ya hahu, dza vizho zha mara ya kwandza. 45 26:45 Joh 12:23; 13:1; 17:1Arihogonya kuvoya, akiauyira o anafundzie akiambira, “Muchere kulala na kudzioyeza? Lolani wakathi wa Mwana wa Mudamu kusalitiwa kwa enye dambi udzafika. 46 Lamukani fwende, kwani ye andiyenisaliti akudzani.”
Jesu anagwirwa
Mariko 14:43-50; Luka 22:47-53; Johana 18:3-12
47 Bai arihokala achere kunena, lola Juda mumwenga wa o anafundzie kumi na airi akidza na kundi bomu ra at'u mariokala madzatsukula mishu na njogoma. At'u aa were mala kwa alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi. 48 Iye musaliti were waap'a muv̱ano kukala, “Ye nindiyemudonera nde mumumalaye, mugwireni.” 49 Juda achenda mara mwenga na akiamba, “Dheri narikale kwako Mwalimu,” gonya akimudonera. 50 Jesu arihoona viryahu, akimwamba Juda, “Henda zho vidzizhokufisha musenangu.” Bai o at'u makimwangiza magandzani Jesu. 51 Lola mumwenga kahi za o at'u mariokala na Jesu, akitsolola mushuwe na akimuthema muhumiki wa ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye akimutosa sikiro. 52 26:52 Mwan 9:6Jesu arihoona viryahu, akimwamba ye mwenye mushu, “Wudzye chowani mwakwe wo mushuwo. Kwani kila ahumiraye mushu, andalagwa kwa mushu. 53 K'umanya kukala nikamwambira Baba anirehere maalifu ga malaika madze mafuhehere, ananirehera mara mwenga? 54 Ela nikihendazho, go Maoro gaambago kukala ni hatha vivi vikale gandathimiradze?”
55 26:55 Luk 19:47Bai Jesu arihogonya kunena vizho, akiagalukira o at'u akiamba, “Mbona mudzambola na mishu na njogoma kudzanigwira dza here mudzaogwira jambazi? Kila siku nidzakala nikikelesi kahi za ro Hekalu kufundisha na k'amunigwirire? 56 26:56 Math' 26:31; Luk 24:46Ela gaga gosi ganakala kwamba garyahu garigoorwa ni manabii gathimire.” Bai o anafundzie osi makimuricha makichimbira.
Jesu a mbere za Baraza ra athumia
Mariko 14:53-65; Luka 22:54-55, 63-71; Johana 18:13-14, 19-24
57 Bai aryahu at'u mariomugwira Jesu, mamuhala vivii hatha nyumbani kwa Kayafa, ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye, mana kuko ndo o alimu a Sheria, na athumia marikokala madzathunganana kumuthariza. 58 Marihokala makwendani, P'et'ero wamuthuwa Jesu kwa kure. Wakwenda hatha achendangira muhalani ha ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye, akikelesi na o arindzi ili alolere chamba vindakaladze. 59 Bai aryahu alombi abomu na ro Baraza ra athumia, majeza zhomu kumala ushahidhi wowosi wa ulongo wa kuthosha kumwalagira Jesu, 60 ela k'amaupatire. At'u anji majeza kumboza ushahidhi wao wa ulongo, ela k'aufwahire kit'u. Mwisho kamare, kuchambola anaalume airi 61 26:61 27:40; Joh 2:19; Mahe 6:14makiamba, “Mwanamulume yuyu waamba kukala ro Hekalu ra Mulungu anadima kuribanda-banda na akariaka kwa siku t'ahu.” 62 Bai ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye akirungarara, na akimuza Jesu akiamba, “Mbona k'udzigidzya kit'u. Zho manenazho ano at'u ni jeri?” 63 26:63 Akinyamala zii: Isa 53:7; Math' 27:12-14; Mwana wa Mulungu: Math' 14:33; 27:40, 43; Luk 22:70Ela Jesu akinyamala zii. Ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye akinena kaheri akiamba, “Ninakulahiza kahi za dzina ra Mulungu ariye moyo, ufwambire chamba uwe ndiwe Masihi Mwana wa Mulungu.” 64 26:64 Dani 7:13; Math' 22:44; 24:30; Mar 13:26; Luk 21:27; Zabu 110:1Jesu akimwamba, “Udzanena. Nami namwambira ujeri, kula vivi mundamona Mwana wa Mudamu akelesi mukono wa kurya wa Mulungu mwenye nguvu, na anadza kuno a dzulu za maingu.” 65 26:65 Kutharura nguwo: Hesab 14:6; Ezra 9:3; Kufuru: Alaw 24:16; Joh 19:7Kusikira vizho thu, ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye akigwira nguwo za kwakwe akizitharura kwa kutsukirwa, kuno ananena na kuamba, “Iyo ni kufuru kamare iyo. K'amudzamusikira zho? Vino funa haja ya mashahidhi angine? 66 Hedu munaambadze nwi?” Na osini makiamba, “Anav̱irya kwalagwa.” 67 26:67 Isa 50:6; 53:5Ndo makimutsuhira mahe ga uso na kumupiga makonde. Angine makimupiga makofi, 68 26:68 21:11kuno manamwamba, “We Masihi, fwambire adzekupiga ni h'ani kala u nabii jeri!”
P'et'ero anamukana Jesu
Mariko 14:66-72; Luka 22:56-62; Johana 18:15-18, 25-27
69 Wakathi uu wosi P'et'ero were akelesi ko muhalani. Bai arihokala kuko muhendzi wa kazi wa kiche wa ye Mulombi mubomu mwenye wakudzamwamba P'et'ero, “We nawe samba were u hamwenga na yuyu Jesu wa Galilaya?” 70 P'et'ero akikana mbere zao akiamba, “Maneno gago mino sigamanya.”
71 Kula haho, P'et'ero akimbola achenda ko muv̱iryani. Anafika kuko, musichana mumwenga akimona na akiambira o at'u mariokala haho kukala, “Yuyu naye were a hamwenga na Jesu wa Nazareth'i benwi.” 72 P'et'ero akikanaiza kaheri kwa kuapa akiamba, “Mwanamulume iye simumanya kamare.” 73 Ela k'avitoere, o at'u mariokala maimire haho makimwamba P'et'ero, “Uwe be unakana ulongo, ela u mumwenga waho. Hatha yo minenereyo inanyesa t'i.” 74 P'et'ero akikwatya kulani na kulaha, akiamba, “Mwanamulume iye be simumanya.” Na haho henye jogolo rikiika. 75 26:75 26:34P'et'ero akikumbukira viryahu arizhoambwa ni Jesu kukala, “Kabila jogolo k'aridzaika undanikana k'ana t'ahu.” Bai achambola ko ndze achendarira na utsungu munji.
