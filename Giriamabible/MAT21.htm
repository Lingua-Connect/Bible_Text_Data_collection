21
Jesu anangira Jerusalemu
Mariko 11:1-11; Luka 19:28-38; Johana 12:12-19
1 Vikara Jesu na anafundzie marihokala ma hehi na Jerusalemu, mafika Beth'ifage, kuko Murima wa Mizeituni. Bai marihofika kuko, Jesu akihuma anafundzie airi akiamba, 2 “Endani hatha mwangire ko kadzidzi mudzikoerekezana nako. Mukifika thu mundaona ndzowe na mwanawe madzafungwa. Avuguleni munirehere. 3 Mut'u yoyosi akimuza mwambireni kukala, ‘Bwana ana haja nao.’ Naye andaarichira mara mwenga.” 4 Bai gosi gahendeka ili garyahu maneno garigonenwa ni nabii gathimire. Kwani nabii mumwenga wanena akiamba,
5 21:5 Isa 62:11; Zaka 9:9“Ambireni at'u a mudzi wa Sayuni kukala,
‘Lolani, muthawali wenu anamudzirira.
Ni mup'ore na adzakwera dzulu za ndzowe,
kidza mwana wa ndzowe.’ ”
6 K'uzhona o anafundzi makiuka na machendahenda here marizhokala madzalagizwa ni Jesu. 7 Makimureha ye ndzowe na mwanawe, makihala maamba gao makihandika ho dzulu zao na Jesu akikwera. 8 21:8 2 Athaw 9:13At'u anji makihala maamba gao makigahandika mo ngirani, angine o makithema vitai zha mihi makivihandika mo ngirani. 9 21:9 Zabu 118:26; 148:1; Math' 20:30-31Bai go makundi ga at'u mariokala mbere na mariokala nyuma osi makikwacha kuimba kwa nguvu makiamba,
“Naalikwe ye mwana wa Daudi!
Naahaswe iye adzaye kwa dzina ra Bwana!
Naalikwe ye Mulungu ariye dzulu!”
10 Jesu arihongira Jerusalemu, mudzi wosi wadudumuka na at'u makiuza, “Ni h'ani ye?” 11 21:11 16:14; 21:46; 26:68; Luk 7:16Bai o at'u mariokala na Jesu makidzigidzya makiamba, “Ni Jesu, yuyahu nabii wa kula Nazareth'i kuko Galilaya.”
Jesu anainga at'u kula kahi za ro Hekalu
Mariko 11:15-19; Luka 19:45-48; Johana 2:13-22
12 Jesu arihongira kahi za ro Hekalu, waainga at'u osi mariokala makigula na kuguza vit'u mo Hekaluni. Kidza akihala zo meza za abandi a p'esa, na vihi zha aguzi a magiya, akivipetula-petula zhosi. 13 21:13 Isa 56:7; Jere 7:11Gonya akiamba, “Zhaorwa kukala, ‘Nyumba yangu indaifwa nyumba ya mavoyo,’ ela ninwi munaihendya p'anga ya kuzama aivi.” 14 Bai at'u anji mariokala k'amaona, na vidende makimuthuwa Jesu mo Hekaluni, naye akidzaahoza. 15 21:15 20:30-31Ela vikara o alombi abomu na alimu a Sheria marihoona go maut'u mabomu arigokala akigahenda Jesu, na zho ahoho marizhokala manapiga londzo na kuamba, “Mup'eni nguma Mwana wa Daudi!” mo Hekaluni makitsukirwa sana. 16 21:16 Zabu 8:2Bai makimuza Jesu makiamba, “Unaasikira o ahoho zho manenazho?” Akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Ee ninaasikira. Ela go Maoro gaambago, ‘Wapata nguma kwa makanwa ga ahoho athithe na ana atsanga’ k'amudzagashoma?”
17 Naye arihogonya kunena vizho akimwambola mumo Jerusalemu achendalala mudzi wa Beth'ania.
Jesu analani mutini
Mariko 11:12-14, 20-24
18 Kunadzacha, Jesu warauka na madzach'a akikwacha kuuya Jerusalemu. Bai mumo ngirani akisikira ndzala. 19 21:19 Luk 13:6Akiona mutini ho k'anda-k'anda za ngira. Achendaulola chamba una matunda, ela k'apatire, mana were u gi makodza mahuhu. Jesu akiulani uryahu muhi akiamba, “Nawe k'undazhala matunda kaheri.” Nawo haho na haho kare ukiuma. 20 K'uzhona o anafundzi marihoona viryahu machangalala zhomu, makiamba, “Muhi uno be tha udzanyaladze haraka vino?” 21 21:21 17:20; Luk 17:6Ela Jesu akiadzigidzya akiamba, “Ninamwambira ujeri kukala, mukikuluhira bila kukala na k'ai-k'ai mundahenda ut'u dza uu nidziwouhenda, na hatha zaidhi. Mundadima hatha kuwambira uu murima, ‘Ng'oka ukagwe baharini.’ Na vindakala vizho zhenye. 22 21:22 7:7Mukikala na kuluhiro mundahokera kila mundichokivoya.”
Jesu anauzwa dzulu za wadimi ahumirao
Mariko 11:27-33; Luka 20:1-8
23 Bai Jesu akiuya kahi za ro Hekalu na akikwatya kufundisha. Arihokala akufundishani, alombi abomu na athumia a Kiyahudi makidzamuza makimwamba, “Unahenda maut'u gano kwa wadimi wani? Na ni h'ani ariyekup'a wadimi uno?” 24 Jesu akiamba, “Hatha mimi nindamuza swali, mukiridzigidzya, nindamwambira ni wadimi wani nihenderao maut'u gaga. 25 Wo wadimi ariohumira Johana kubat'izira at'u walahi? Wala kwa Mulungu hedu wala kwa anadamu?” O alombi abomu na o athumia makiojana makiamba, “Fukiamba wala kwa Mulungu, andafuza, ‘Kwanoni k'amumukuluhirire?’ 26 21:26 14:5Na hukiamba, ‘Wala kwa mudamu,’ fu hatharini, mana aa at'u osini manakuluhira kukala Johana were ni nabii.” 27 Kwa vizho makimwamba Jesu, “K'ahumanya.” Jesu naye akiamba, “Hatha mimi nami sindamwambira wo wadimi nihumirao kuhendera maut'u gaga.”
Fumbo ra athana airi mariohumwa ni babiyao
28 21:28-31 7:2121:28 Luk 15:11Jesu arihogonya kuadzigidzya o alombi abomu na o athumia, waambozera mufano akiamba, “Nambirani ut'u umwenga hedu. Kwakala na mut'u mumwenga ariyekala na athana airi. Siku mwenga akimwamba ye muthana mukulu, ‘Rero enda ukarime ko munda wa mizabibu.’ 29 Mwana anambola kunena akiamba, ‘Aa sindakwenda mino.’ Ela gonya ye mukulu akigalukwa ni moyo hatha achenda. 30 Kula haho, ye bimut'u achenda kwa ye wa hiri achendamwamba, ‘Rero enda ukarime ko munda wa mizabibu.’ Ye mwana akiamba, ‘H'aya ndakwenda.’ Ela k'endere bule. 31 21:31 6:10Vikara kahi za ano airi ni hiye ariyehenda malagizo ga babaye?” Makiamba, “Ni iye mukulu.” Jesu akiamba, “H'aya, hatha mimi nami namwambira ujeri wenye kukala athozi a kodi na malaya manangira kahi za uthawali wa Mulungu mbere zenu. 32 21:32 Luk 7:29-30Kwani Johana Mubat'izaji wakudzamwanyesa ngira ya hachi, ela k'amumukuluhirire, athozi a kodi na malaya o makimukuluhira. Nwi ng'o kamare, hatha bada ya kuona kukala andziyenu madzamukuluhira kidza nwi k'amugalukirwe ni mioyo.”
Fumbo ra ak'urima athiriri
Mariko 12:1-12; Luka 20:9-19
33 21:33 Isa 5:1-2Bai Jesu arihogonya kunena vizho akiamba, “Sirikizani nimwambire fumbo ringine bai. Kwakala na mwenye nyumba mumwenga ariyerima mundawe akiulaya mizabibu, akiudengereshera wiyo, akitsimba wina wa kukamulira zabibu, gonya akiaka uringo wa kurindira. Kugonya vizho, akiukodisha kwa ak'urima, gonya ye mwenye achenda ts'i nyingine. 34 Makathi ga mavuno garihofika, waahuma ahumikie mende makagerwe muthalowe wa zabibu ni o ak'urima. 35 21:35 22:6Bai o ak'urima marihoaona o ahumiki, mahala mumwenga makimupiga, mumwenga makimukot'a mawe, na mumwenga makimwalaga. 36 Bai, ye mwenye munda akihuma ahumiki angine, anji kukira o arioahuma kwandza. Ela hatha ao nao k'amapatire kit'u, kwani mahendwa dza zho zha andziyao. 37 Ye mwenye munda arihoona vizho akiamba, ‘Charo kiki huriza nimuhume mwanangu. Bila shaka iye mandamuishimu.’ 38 Ela o ak'urima marihomona ye mwana akudzani, makiamba, ‘Yuno be hatha se andiyeuhala ufwa uno munda? Alume nahumwalage yuyu, uu munda ukale wehu.’ 39 21:39 Aebu 13:12Makimuhala kare makimwamboza ndze ya wo munda makimwalaga. 40 Vikara, munaona ye mwenye munda akidza andaahendadze arya ak'urima?” 41 21:41 22:7Makidzigidzya makiamba, “Andaahala o ak'urima aalage, gonya wo munda aap'e ak'urima angine mandiokala makimup'a muthalowe wa zabibu kila mavunaho.” 42 21:42 Zabu 118:22-23Jesu akiamba, “Hedu k'amudzangwe kugashoma go Maoro gaambago,
‘Ro iwe ririrokahalwa ni aaki
vivi be ridzagaluka ndo iwe bomu ra p'embeni.
Ut'u uwo wahendwa ni Bwana,
Na fukiulola unafwangalaza?’
43 21:43-44 Dani 2:34-35, 44-45Kwa vizho be namwambira kamare kukala, wo uthawali wa Mulungu mundauhokwa na ugerwe at'u mandioishi maisha ga kumufwahiza Mulungu. 44 Na iye andiyerigwerera iwe riro andabandika-bandika. Ela mut'u yoyosi rindiyemugwerera, rindamusaga tikitiki.”
45 O alombi abomu na o Mafarisayo marihosikira go mafumbo ga Jesu, makimanya kukala manaambwa ao. 46 21:46 21:11; 26:4-5Kwa vizho makijeza kumugwira mamushithaki, ela makishindwa kwa kukala were manaagoha o at'u. Kwani o at'u were manamona Jesu ni nabii.
