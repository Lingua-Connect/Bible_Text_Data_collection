23
Jesu anahirikwa kwa P'ilato
Math'ayo 27:1-2, 11-14; Mariko 15:1-5; Johana 18:28-38
1 Ndo ro kundi rosi ra at'u rikiungarara na rikimuthongozha Jesu muhaka kwa luwali P'ilato. 2 23:2 20:20-26Marihofika kuko, makikwatya kumushithaki na kuamba, “Mut'u yuyu fwamugwira anaavuga akili at'u ehu. Kidza hatha anaambira at'u kukala masimboze kodi kwa Kaisari na kuno anadziamba kukala iye nde ye Masihi, yani muthawali.”
3 Kwa vizho P'ilato arihosikira vizho akimuza Jesu akiamba, “Vidze we ndiwe muthawali wa Ayahudi?” Jesu akimudzigidzya akimwamba, “Uwe udzanena.” 4 Na P'ilato akiagalukira o alombi abomu na ro kundi ra at'u na akiambira, “Mut'u yuyu simona na kosa rorosi ra kumuhendya ahukumiwe.” 5 Kusikira vizho, o at'u makikaza kamare kuamba, “Mut'u yuyu anavuga akili at'u kukirira na go mafundishoge na kuahenda mathinye bugwa kahi za Judea ndzima. Ut'u uu wauandza Galilaya na vino hatha adzavifisha kuku kamare.”
Jesu anahirikwa kwa Herode
6 P'ilato arihosikira vivi waauza o at'u chamba ye mut'u madzemushithaki ni Mugalilaya. 7 23:7 3:1Ndo arihombirwa kukala Jesu anambola kahi za jimbo rithawalwaro ni Herode, akiona baha alagize ahirikwe kwa iye Herode, ariye wakathi uwo were a mo Jerusalemu.
8 23:8 9:9K'uzhona zho Herode arihomona Jesu wafwahirwa zhomu kwa kukala kwa muda mure were anathamani kumona. Naye kwa kukala were wasikira manji dzuluze were ana thamani kumona anahenda miujiza. 9 Kwa vizho Herode arihomona Jesu, wamuza-muza maswali manji, ela Jesu k'amudzigidzyire bule. 10 Kuno ko, o alombi abomu na alimu a Sheria maimire haho na manasita kuamba Jesu ana makosa.
11 23:11 Math' 27:28; Mar 15:17; Joh 19:2Ndo Herode na shikarie makikwatya kumuv̱udhya na kumuzuma Jesu. Gonya makimuvika rivalo ridzo ra kithawali na makimulazha aujwe kwa luwali P'ilato. 12 Kula siku iyo Herode na P'ilato makigwirana usena, mana haho nyuma-nyuma were be k'amapatana kamare.
Jesu anahukumiwa kwalagwa
Math'ayo 27:15-26; Mariko 15:6-15; Johana 18:39–19:16
13 Bai marihofika kwa P'ilato, P'ilato waaiha hamwenga o alombi abomu na o vilongozi a o at'u, 14 na akiambira vivi, “At'u ninwi mudzanirehera mut'u yuyu ambaye munaamba anaavuga akili at'u na kuhenda mathinye bugwa. Nidzamuza maswali mbere zenu na lola nikamona kukala kahi za gago gosi mudzigomushithaki nago, be k'ahana kosa rorosi arirohenda. 15 Hatha Herode naye kidza k'amonere na kosa ndosa akamudzya kuku kwehu. Kwa vizho k'ahendere ut'u wowosi uwi wa kumuhendya alagwe. 16 Nindizhohenda ni kulagiza apigwe vikotho gonya arichizwe.”
[ 17 Vikara kahi za kila sherehe ya P'asaka, P'ilato were k'ana budhi kuavugulira mufungwa mumwenga.] 18 23:18-25 Mahe 3:13-14Ela o at'u makipiga mikululu na kuamba, “Yuyu mwalage! Fuvugulire ye Baraba!” 19 (Baraba were ni mut'u ariyefungwa jela kwa kukala wakalamo kahi za bugwa ririrohendeka mo Jerusalemu na kahi za maut'u ga kulaga at'u.)
20 P'ilato were ahendzazho ni kumurichiza Jesu, kwa vizho akijaribu kunena na ro kundi ra at'u kaheri, 21 ela o hatha makithuwa o wa, “Musulubishe! Musulubishe!” 22 P'ilato arihosikira vizho akiuza lwa hahu akiamba, “Asulubiwe kwa adzakosani kibomu? Nidzamulola na nikamona k'ana kosa ra hatha kukala ahukumiwe kwalagwa, kwa vizho ndalagiza apigwe vikotho na gonya arichizwe.” 23 Ela o at'u makikaza kamare kupiga mikululu na kuamba, asulubiwe. Na lola mwisho zo k'ululu zao zikishinda. 24 Kwa vizho P'ilato akimuhukumu kwalagwa ye Jesu here marizhokala manahendza. 25 Ndo akivugulirwa Baraba ariye were wafungwa kwa kuthinya bugwa na kwalaga, naye were nde mahendzaye avugulwe. Gonya P'ilato akimuhala Jesu na akimulazha kwao here marizhokala manamala.
Jesu anasulubiwa
Math'ayo 27:31-44; Mariko 15:21-32; Johana 19:17-27
26 Kwa vizho o shikari a Kirumi makimuhala Jesu machenda naye mende makamwalage. Bai marihokala makwendani makithungana mut'u aifwaye Simoni Muk'irene. Mut'u yuyu were anangira mo mudzini kula mashambani. O shikari marihomona mamugwira na makimuhika wo musalaba kithuroni, autsukule, kuno Jesu a mbere ye a nyuma.
27 Kidza ngororoki bomu ya at'u yakala inamuthuwa Jesu nyuma-nyuma. Kahi za ii ngororoki mwere muna anaache mariokala manapiga k'ululu na kurira kwa ut'u wa go garigokala ganamukalira Jesu. 28 Jesu arihoona vizho waagalukira o anaache na akiambira, “H'enwi ano m'ama a Jerusalemu, at'u ninwi musirireni kwa ut'u wa kunishikithikira mimi, ela dziririreni ninwi enye hamwenga na ana enu. 29 23:29 21:23Kwani lolani siku za thabu zinadza ambaho at'u mandaamba, ‘Baha ao anaache mario ni t'asa, baha ao mario k'amazhalire ahoho, hedu hatha kuamwisa ana!’ 30 23:30 Hose 10:8; Ugun 6:16Gonya mandanena na mirima na mayambe, ‘Nwi mirima h'aya fugwerereni!’ Na zho virima vithithe-vithithe mandazhambira, ‘Hedu hufinikireni!’ 31 23:31 1 P'et 4:17Kwani ikikala at'u manadima kuhenda maut'u gaga kwa muhi mwitsi vidze makivihenda kwa muhi mufu vindakaladze?”
32 Kidza were hana ahendi a mai airi, bai aa nao mathongozhwa hamwenga na Jesu kwendalagwa. 33 23:33 Isa 53:12Ndoo vivii, hatha hat'u haifwaho Ribeshe ra Kitswa, bai haho makimusulubisha Jesu. Naye wasulubiwa hamwenga na ahendi a mai airi, mumwenga mukono wa kurya, na mungine mukono wa kumots'o. [ 34 23:34 Zabu 22:18; Math' 5:44; Luk 6:28; Mahe 7:60Ndo Jesu akiamba, “Baba asamehe! Mana k'amamanya ro mahendaro.”] O shikari makizigazha zo nguwo za Jesu. Nao mahenda vivi kwa kuhenda kuzipigira k'ura. 35 23:35-39 Zabu 22:7; 109:35Na o at'u makikala ma haho manavilolera zho vikalazho, kuno ko o vilongozi a Kiyahudi makikala manamusholera Jesu na kuamba, “H'aya k'aokola angine, vivi be ni adziokole mwenye kala nde iye Masihi jeri ariyetsagulwa ni Mulungu.”
36 23:36 Zabu 69:21Na o shikari nao makihenda dza vizho zhenye mamuv̱udhya Jesu, kwani mamuthuwa hehi na makimup'a siki, 37 23:37-38 Math' 27:11makimwamba, “H'aya dziokole mwenye kala u muthawali wa Ayahudi jeri!”
38 Kidza ho dzulu za kitswache hakotw'a kibao kirichokala chaorwa maneno gaga: Yuyu Nde Muthawali Wa Ayahudi.
39 Vikara mumwenga kahi za o ahendi a mai ariyekala adzanganywa haho, wamusholera Jesu akimwamba, “Vidze we siwe ye Masihi bule? H'aya dziokole mwenye gonya ufuokole naswi!”
40 Ye mungine ye arihosikira vizho akimudumulira ye mwandziwe akiamba, “We fuambe kumwogoha Mulungu bule? Siswi hosini ahahu fwahukumiwa vimwenga. 41 Na bai siswi zho furizhohukumiwa ni karakara, kwani zho fuhendwazho be vinalengana na go mai furigogahenda, ela yuyu be k'ahendere rii rorosi.” 42 Gonya akimwamba Jesu, “Nikumbukira bai Jesu, undihokudza kahi za uthawaliwo!” 43 23:43 2 Akor 12:3Jesu akimudzigidzya akiamba, “Nakwambira ujeri wenye, vivi rero kare fundakwenda hosini p'ehoni.”
Jesu anamarigizika
Math'ayo 27:45-56; Mariko 15:33-41; Johana 19:28-30
44 23:44-45 Amo 8:9Ganakala gago were ni saa t'andahu, bai ts'i yosi yagit'ana kiza muhadhi saa chenda, 45 mana ro dzuwa raricha kuala na yo p'azia ya nguwo kahi za ro Hekalu ikitharuka h'waa p'ande mbiri. 46 23:46 Zabu 31:5; Mahe 7:59Gonya Jesu akipiga k'ululu akiamba, “Baba, yo roho yangu ninayika mikononi mwako.” Naye ko kugonya kunena vizho thu, akimarigizika kare. 47 23:47 K'ana makosa: Math' 27:19; Mahe 3:14; 7:52; 22:14Bai ye mubomu wa shikari arihoona vizho virizho were vidzahendeka wamulika Mulungu na akiamba, “Ni jeri mut'u yuyu were k'ana makosa gogosi.” 48 Vikara ro kundi ra at'u mario were madzirelolera zho vikalazho marihoona go maut'u gago gakihendeka, mauya midzini kwao kuno manathukusa vitswa zhao kwa kushikithika. 49 23:49 8:2-3; Zabu 38:11Na o at'u mario were ni asena a Jesu, ambao angine were ni aryahu anaache mariokala madzamuthuwa Jesu kula Galilaya, maima kure vichache na makilolera maut'u gaga.
Kuzikwa kwa Jesu
Math'ayo 27:57-61; Mariko 15:42-47; Johana 19:38-42
50-51 Vikara kwereko na mut'u mumwenga ariyeifwa Josefu naye were anambola mudzi mumwenga wa Ayahudi uifwao Arimath'ea. Iye were ni mut'u mudzo kidza ni muhachi, na were anauthariza uthawali wa Mulungu. Idzaho were ni mumwenga waho kahi za ro Baraza ra athumia, iye k'aaungire mukono kahi za gago marigogahenda. 52 Bai wakwenda hatha kwa P'ilato na achendaumala mwiri wa Jesu akauzike. 53 Arihoruhusiwa wakwendamuthinya akimulinga-linga ts'andza ya kitani, na achendamwika kahi za mbira iriyohenda kutsongwa iweni. Mbira ii were k'aidzahumirwa bule. 54 Ganakala gago were ni siku ya Mathayarisho, mana siku ya Sabato were i hehi na kwangira. 55 23:55-56 8:2-3Na aryahu anaache mariokala mamuthuwa Jesu hangu Galilaya, makwenda na Josefu na machendayona yo mbira na makiona viryahu mwiriwe urizhoikwa. 56 23:56 Kumbo 20:10Ndo makiuya midzini kwao na machendathayarisha mavumba na marashi ga kudzaumwagira mwiri wa Jesu. Na irihofika siku ya Sabato makioya here Sheria iambazho.
