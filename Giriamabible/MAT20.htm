20
Jesu analazha fumbo ra vibaruwa a mundani
1 “Kwani uthawali wa mulunguni udzahalana na mwenye nyumba ariyekala na munda wa mizabibu. Mut'u iye warauka madzach'a maitsi genye kwendamala vibaruwa mende makamuhendere kazi mundani kwakwe. 2 Bai arihoapata makisikizana kukala andaariha dinari mwenga kutswa. Marihogonya kusikizana akiahirika ko mundani kwakwe. 3 Inapiga saa t'ahu achendathekeza at'u angine mariokala makelesi ho sokoni thu na kazi ya kuhenda k'amana. 4 Ye mwenye munda akiambira, ‘Hatha ninwi nanwi hindeni mukanihendere kazi kuryahu mundani kwangu, nami nindamurihat'o.’ Nao machenda jeri. 5 Bai vivi wavithuwa hatha mara mbiri, murongo wa saa t'andahu na murongo wa saa chenda. 6 Saa kumi na mwenga irihokala i hehi achenda ko sokoni kaheri, na achendadulukiza at'u mariokala madzadzikelesira thu. Akiauza, ‘Kwanoni hangu kudzecherera mukelesi thu?’ 7 O at'u makimudzigidzya makimwamba, ‘Fundakosadze kukelesi naswi k'ahudzaona mut'u wa kuhup'a kazi?’ Ye mwenye munda akiamba, ‘Andziyenu kinawaka kare ko mundani, kwa vizho endani mukagwirane nao.’
8 20:8 Alaw 19:13; Kumbu 24:15“Bai, at'u aryahu mahenda kazi vivii hatha kukitswa. K'ona yuyahu mwenye munda akimulagiza munyaparawe akaihe o ahendzi a kazi adze aarihe. Bai, waandza na aryahu arioapata mwisho, hatha akimarigiza na aryahu arioapata kwandza. 9 Vikara aryahu at'u mariohirikwa ko mundani saa kumi na mwenga marihokudza, makirifwa dinari mwenga-mwenga. 10 Aryahu at'u mariohirikwa ko mundani kwandza marihokudza, makiona here kwamba mandangezwa, ela k'avikalire vizho bule, ao nao makirifwa dinari mwenga-mwenga. 11 Vikara marihoona kukala madzarifwa karakara na o angine, machendamulalamikira ye mwenye munda makiamba, 12 ‘Siswi fudzahenda kazi hangu madzach'a hatha vivi. Dzuwa rosi ridzagomera mwirini mwehu, gonya unafuriha karakara na aa madziohenda kazi kwa saa mwenga thu-thu-thu.’ 13 Ye mwenye munda akimudzigidzya mumwenga wao akiamba, ‘Musena, ro kosa nidziromuhendera nwi ni hiro? Samba fudzasikizana kukala nimurihe dinari mwenga kutswa nwi? 14 Mimi mwenye nidzaona nimurihe ninwi a kwandza karakara na o a mwisho, kwa vizho halani p'esa zenu mwende. 15 Namanya muna wivu kwa wo udzo nidziwouhenda. Ela ni lazima mumanye kukala nina uhuru wa kuhumira p'esa zangu nihendzazho.’ ”
16 20:16 19:30Bai Jesu arihogonya kuthala ro fumbo riro akiamba, “Kwa vizho bai a kwandza mandakala a mwisho, na a mwisho mandakala a kwandza.”
Jesu anathala lwa hahu viryahu andizhofwa
Mariko 10:32-34; Luka 18:31-34
17 20:17-19 16:21; 27:26-31Vikara Jesu na anafundzie marihokala ma ngirani kwenda Jerusalemu, waavuha k'anda o anafundzie kumi na airi akiamba, 18 “Lolani, vivi hunenda Jerusalemu. Na kuko Mwana wa Mudamu andalazhwa kwa alombi abomu na alimu a Sheria, nao mandamuhukumu kwalagwa. 19 Kidza ao nao mandamulazha kwa at'u mario si Ayahudi mamusholere, mamupige, gonya mamusulubishe, ela siku ya hahu andafufulwa.”
Mukaza Zebedayo anamuvoya Jesu dzulu za anae
Mariko 10:35-45
20 Vikara kahi za wakathi uryahu, mameye Johana na Jakobu ana a Zebedayo, wakwenda kwa Jesu hamwenga na anae. Anafika kuko, akimupigira mavindi Jesu akimwamba, “Bwana, nifwaha na ut'u umwenga.” 21 Jesu akimuza akimwamba, “Nikufwahe na noni?” Ye mwanamuche akimwamba, “Undihokwacha kuthawala, nakuvoya, uruhusu kukala, mwanangu mumwenga akelesi mukonowo wa kurya na mungine akelesi mukonowo wa kumots'o.” 22 20:22 26:39; Mar 14:36; Luk 22:42; Joh 18:11Ela Jesu akimugalukira Jakobu na Johana akiamba, “Ninwi be tha k'amukimanya bule cho mukivoyacho. Vidze cho kikombe*20:22 Kikombe haha manaye ni mateso. ninwacho munadima kukinwa nwi?” Nao makiamba, “Ee funadima.” 23 20:23 Mahe 12:2Bai marihonena vizho Jesu akiamba, “Ee kikombe changu mundakinwa. Ela kumutsagula yuya akelesi mukono wa kurya, yuya mukono wa kumots'o, sina wadimi nazho bule. Kwani kuko, Baba wakutengezera at'ue, na ao ndo mandiokala kuko.” 24 Vikara o anafundzi kumi marihovisikira, makiatsukirirwa o ndugu airi. 25 Ela Jesu akiaiha osini akiamba, “Munamanya kukala athawali a at'u mario si Ayahudi manahendza kuthawala at'u ao na ukali munji. Na aryahu abomu mario ts'ini zao nao, manahendza kuhumira wadimi wao wosi kuatumisha o at'u. 26 20:26-27 23:11; Mar 9:35; Luk 9:48Ela ninwi musikale vizho bule. Iye ahendzaye kukala mubomu suthi akale muhumiki wenu. 27 Na kila ahendzaye kukala kitswa kahi zenu, suthi akale mutumwa wenu. 28 20:28 Isa 53:12; 1 Akor 6:20; 7:23; Agal 1:4; 1 T'imo 2:5-6; 1 P'et 1:18-19Kwani hatha Mwana wa Mudamu naye k'adzire haha urumwenguni adze ahumikirwe, ela wakudza ahumike na alazhe uzimawe aakombole at'u anji.”
Jesu anahoza vipofu airi
Mariko 10:46-52; Luka 18:35-43
29 20:29-34 9:27-30Badaye, Jesu na anafundzie marihokala manambola mudzi wa Jeriko, kundi bomu ra at'u rambola hamwenga nao rikiathuwa. 30 20:30-31 1:1; 9:27; 12:23; 15:22; 21:9, 15; 22:41-46; Mar 10:47-48; 11:10; Luk 1:32, 69; 2:11; 18:38-39; Joh 7:42Vikara were kuna vipofu airi mariokala makelesi k'anda za yo barabara. Lola marihosikira kukala Jesu anakira, maluta mimiro makiamba, “Mwana wa Daudi huonere mbazi!” 31 O at'u makiadumulira makiamba manyamale, ela o hatha makikaza kuluta mimiro makiamba, “Bwana, mwana wa Daudi huonere mbazi!” 32 Jesu akiima, akiaiha akiamba, “H'aya munamala nimuhendereni?” 33 O vipofu makiamba, “Bwana, funamala ugunule matso gehu.” 34 Bai Jesu akiaonera mbazi akiagut'a go matso gao, na haho henye makikwacha kuona na makimuthuwa.
